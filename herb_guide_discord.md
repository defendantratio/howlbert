all herbs below are forageable with `/field action:forage`. inventory key format: `herb_name`. use `/medic action:treat herb:herb_key` to apply.

cures: mild poison, poison ivy rash, partial blindness
⚠ toxic in excess — repeated use causes addiction, aggression, and paranoia

`herb_tansy` · `herb_watermint` · `herb_mugwort` · `herb_catmint` · `herb_pine_needle` · `herb_shepherds_purse` · `herb_saffron` · `herb_slippery_elm` · `herb_parsley` (large doses)
