the wolves

sixty-three canon wolves, four packs, and a hungry god that watches them all.

the wolf-world holds wolves of every rank and stripe: alphas who rule through fear or patience, medics who carry the green tongue's darkest knowledge, pups who dream of glory, and rogues who survive by teeth and cunning. these are their stories.

greyspire

the mountain pack. hard, cold, and unforgiving. these wolves are the teeth of the maw, sharpened on the bones of the weak. strength is truth. blood is law.
