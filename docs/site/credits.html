the developer

howlbert is the vision and work of one wolf, who pours their heart into every line of code and every piece of lore.

basil
✦ he/him ✦ 20 y/o ✦ neurodivergent ✦ queer ✦ disabled ✦ artist ✦ programmer
basil is the primary developer, worldbuilder, and caretaker of howlbert. he writes the code that brings the wolf-world to life, crafts the herbs and diseases, shapes the blinking, and keeps the den running through every sunrise.

you can find basil in the discord server, usually buried in logs. he is always open to ideas, questions, and stories.

the howlbert team

howlbert is built and maintained by a small, dedicated den of wolves who believe in the power of story and the weight of survival.

  • development – the code, the database, the slash commands, the endless debugging of edge cases. the bones of the bot.
  • lore and worldbuilding – the wolf-world, the four packs, the maw, the cats, the twoleg factions, the herbs, the diseases, the blinking. the fur on the bones.
  • community and moderation – the wolves who keep the den warm, the scenes alive, and the stories flowing.
  • art and design – the icons, the web pages, the visual soul of the wolf-world.

if you would like to contribute, join the discord and raise your voice. the pack grows stronger with every new wolf.

the spirit of the world

the wolf-world is not a game to be won. it is a story to be lived. every hunt, every howl, every wound, every grief is a verse in a song that has been sung since the first wolf opened its eyes under a cold moon. — from the thistlehide oral tradition

howlbert is a place for wolves to be wolves: to struggle, to love, to fail, to rise. the maw is hungry, but so is hope. the packs are fractured, but they can be mended. the world is shrinking, but there is still room for a howl to carry.

thank you for being part of it. thank you for carrying the story forward.
