a whole wolf, rolled in one sitting, name to scars.

this fills a complete, submission-ready character sheet: name, pack, appearance, a backstory seed, skills, weaknesses, and a fair spread of stats. reroll any line you don't like, then flesh out the prose in your own words. it's a spark, not a script.

finishing your wolf

the rolled lines are scaffolding. the three prose fields, appearance, personality, and backstory, are yours to write: expand the appearance to 2 to 3 sentences, turn the traits into a paragraph or two, and grow the backstory seed into at least three paragraphs covering your origin, the wound that shaped you, your stance toward the maw, and a brush with the twolegs. the stats are rolled inside the real range for your rank; shift points to fit the character, then set them with /rpg action:setstats.

/register now genuinely takes appearance directly: fur_color, eye_color, and markings are short fields that show on /profile and get passed down to any pups this wolf has later; appearance, backstory, and rp_sample are longer prose fields that show on /profile sheet:true. all six are optional at registration and can be set or changed anytime after with /character. a genetic condition, if you rolled one above, goes in the separate genetic field, not appearance.
