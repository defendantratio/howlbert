build a wolf

a whole wolf, rolled in one sitting, name to scars.

this fills a complete, submission-ready character sheet: name, pack, appearance, a backstory seed, skills, weaknesses, and a fair spread of stats. reroll any line you don't like, then flesh out the prose in your own words. it's a spark, not a script, and nothing here is machine-written.

finishing your wolf

the rolled lines are scaffolding. the three prose fields, appearance, personality, and backstory, are yours to write: expand the appearance to 2 to 3 sentences, turn the traits into a paragraph or two, and grow the backstory seed into at least three paragraphs covering your origin, the wound that shaped you, your stance toward the maw, and a brush with the twolegs. the stats are a fair starting spread (all sixes average out to a plain wolf); shift points to fit the character, then set them in the bot. when it's ready, submit it for approval and /register.
