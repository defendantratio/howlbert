book one the blinking

a wrongness spreads through the caldera in twelve phases.
every wolf feels the pressure.

this is the mechanical reference for every wolf in the world. each phase carries a den wide pressure, and each named wolf has a lane: a way to act, to resist, to survive, or to fall. the maw chews. the packs blink.

the twelve phases

staff advance the plot when roleplay earns it, not on a fixed day counter. each phase adds a global mechanical weight. the table below shows the phase title, the news that spreads at sunrise, and the active mechanical effect for every wolf.

1. bitten moon
all wolves −1 mood at sunrise. pups are shielded if rime (greyspire) or root (thistlehide) are alive and active.
2. white omen
thistlehide scouts get +1 standing on patrol. quest blink_border_patrol opens.
3. peak bleeds
mountain travel dc +2. greyspire hunt/scavenge +10% bones.
4. warm below
silverrush hydration −2 at sunrise; fishing −25%; fish rot accelerates. pups shielded if vulcan stonehide is active.
5. belly silence
mistmoor disease pressure rises; rot lung can spread. mirewort reduces the chance if active.
6. border paranoia
cat trust −3; sniff border fights +25%; rogue crime lane opens; quest blink_wind_witness.
7. iron debt
forest travel dc +2; pack pact forge dc +2; cross pack steal caught standing −2 extra.
8. mill tooth
explore investigate may yield fossil tooth (+bones, standing). quest blink_mill_scout opens.
9. memory bites
howl costs −3 mood if pack unity < 5. rivershroud can waive this for thistlehide.
10. blame spiral
all great packs unity −1; cat trust −2.
11. ash naming
howl +1 unity; creek drink +5 hydration; quest blink_ash_naming opens.
12. pact remembered
cat trust +5; warm river debuffs end; plot can reset to 0 when staff close book one.

universal participation while plot phase > 0, every wolf can take part. the first plot tagged action each sunrise grants +1 mood and a flavor witness. sniff progresses healer listen (phases 1 to 5) and wind witness (phase 6+). medics and apprentices can progress healer touch (phases 5 to 11) and earn daily standing or mood.
