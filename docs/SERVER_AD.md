[:tooth:]     **WHISPERS** BEYOND THE TREES

> -# A new **long-term** roleplay including **psychological horror** & **religious ambiguity**, for roleplayers aged **18+**.
> -# Follows the struggles of **four packs**: *Greyspire*, *Mistmoor*, *Thistlehide*, & *Silverrush*.

`📜`     STORY **PREMISE**:     __BOOK ONE: THE BLINKING__

> -# ***"Did you taste it?"***
> -# It starts like this:
> -# *The moon has begun to blink.*
> -# Once, there was one pack. Then came hunger, sickness, and the iron teeth of Twolegs. The pack split into four: teeth, belly, fur, and tears. For generations they have watched each other from afar: trading whispers, trading blood, trading silence for survival.
> -# But now the moon rises bruised and bitten. Star-speech rides the wind. Silverrush runs warm; fish belly-up in the shallows. In Mistmoor, the Drown-Sick wait for a chewing sound in the belly that never comes.
> -# At the borders, forest-cat warriors prowl the scent-lines. Logging scars thread toward an old paper mill: iron on every tongue. Something ancient sleeps beneath the timbers.
> -# The packs are afraid. The packs are hungry. And the **Maw has not fed** in a very, very long time.
