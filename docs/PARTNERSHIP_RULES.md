# partnership rules

paste as one pinned post in the partners channel. the short version: we trade ads with servers whose standards match ours, and we say no to the ones that don't. saying no isn't a judgment on them; it's about what we can put our name next to.

---

`🤝`     **partnerships**

> -# we're a **18+ wolf RP** server built on a custom bot, with heavy themes and **no NSFW anywhere**. a partnership means we hand your ad to our members and you hand ours to yours, so we only take partners we'd genuinely point a member at.
> -# partnerships are **free**, permanent until either side ends them, and never bought.

`✅`     **what we look for**

-# **an active, moderated community.** a real staff team, a working report route, and rules that are actually enforced. size matters less than upkeep; a tidy 30-member server beats a dead 3,000.
-# **a real age gate on adult material.** we verify **every** member with a redacted photo ID or a roblox age group screenshot before they get access; no exceptions, no "trust me." if your server carries adult themes, we expect a gate of comparable strength. a self-reported age or a reaction role isn't one.
-# **your own original creative work,** or properly credited work. same standard we hold ourselves to.
-# **a server that would be comfortable hosting our ad,** which names psychological horror, religious ambiguity, disease, and permanent character death.

`⛔`     **what we don't partner with**

> -# **servers that host ERP or NSFW RP at all,** main draw or not. not a judgment on them; adults may do as they like. but our rules say **no NSFW anywhere, instant ban**, and we can't hand our members a server that hosts the exact thing we ban and expect that rule to mean anything. "it isn't our focus" doesn't change that it's there.
> -# **servers with a weak age gate on adult material,** including "18+" by self-report, reaction role, or nothing at all. we ID-verify every member, and we won't point them at a door that opens easier than ours.
> -# **any server that lets minors near adult content.** this is the one we won't discuss.
> -# **AI-generated servers.** if the art, writing, lore, or characters are AI-made, we're not a fit; we're original-work only.
> -# **art theft, tracing, or uncredited reposting** as a normal practice.
> -# **hate speech, harassment, or a public record of ignoring safety reports.** including staff with credible, documented callouts for harm.
> -# **crypto, NFT, or paid-membership-gated RP,** and anything selling mechanical advantage.
> -# **servers under a month old with no members yet.** come back when there's something to partner with.

`📋`     **how it works**

-# **your own text channel, not a shared list or a pin.** we give every partner their own dedicated text channel for their ad, so it stands alone and can't be buried; we ask for the same. not a forum thread, not one message among many in a shared partners channel, not a pin (pins are capped and shared, so a wall of them hides every one). equal placement, because that's exactly what we give you.
-# ads can run **more than one message** if the content needs it; partner ads live in their own channel here, so length isn't a problem.
-# **a dedicated partnership ping, not `@everyone`.** we ping a **partner-ads role** that members opt into, and we ask you to have one too. no `@everyone` or `@here` on either side; a member who wants partner ads can take the role, and one who doesn't shouldn't be woken up for it.
-# if your server has no partnership ping role yet, **make one before we trade ads.** it takes a minute and it's the difference between an ad people read and an ad people mute.
-# **a partner representative is required.** name one contact from your side who can speak for the server; they get the partner rep role here, and we give you one of ours. no rep, no partnership; we won't chase a server with nobody to reach.
-# ads must include a **short pitch, your invite, and the content warnings your themes call for.** ours does; we expect the same. keep it readable; we'll do the same.
-# tell us if your **invite dies or your server changes hands.** a dead link is the usual reason a partnership quietly ends.

`⚖️`     **while you're here**

> -# **partner staff follow our rules like anyone else,** including the NSFW and AI rules, in every channel and DM.
> -# **no DM advertising our members.** ever. this is an instant end to the partnership and a ban, and it's the single most common thing that gets a partner removed.
> -# **no poaching:** don't recruit our players or our staff out of the server you're partnered with.
> -# bring problems to our staff privately, not to our general chat.

`🚪`     **ending a partnership**

-# either side may end it at any time, for any reason, and the ads come down.
-# we'll end it **without notice** for: DM advertising, a rule break by your staff, a safety issue, or content drifting into anything in the "don't partner with" list.
-# ending a partnership isn't drama and we won't make it public. we just take the ad down.

`📬`     **applying**

-# DM a staff member with: your **invite**, rough **member count**, a link to your **rules**, your **age policy**, your **partnership ping role**, and the **rep** we should be talking to.
-# we'll read your rules before answering. if we say no, it's a fit problem, not a verdict on your community.
