# Soot

**Name:** Soot  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Medic (Healer)  
**Age:** ~12 moons (1 year; still growing)  
**Sexuality:** Undiscovered  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

## Appearance

Soot is a small, scruffy wolf with a charcoal-black coat and white socks on all four paws. Her eyes are a striking, mismatched blue (left) and amber (right): a trait that some wolves call a blessing and others call a curse. Her fur is always tangled with burrs and mud, no matter how much she grooms. She has a nervous habit of chewing her tail, which is perpetually bald at the tip.

## Personality

Soot wants to be a healer like Mirewort. She does not care that he is strange, or that the Rotmother suspects him, or that other wolves avoid him. He saved her life when she was a pup (rot-lung, the same sickness that killed her litter). She owes him everything.

She is clumsy: she trips over roots, drops herbs, mixes poultices backward. But she learns. She watches Mirewort's every move, asks questions constantly, and practices on carrion when no one is looking. She will be a good healer someday. She just needs time.

## Backstory

Soot's entire litter died of rot-lung when she was three moons old. She was the only survivor, and she survived because Mirewort sat with her for three days and three nights, dripping tea into her mouth and keeping her fever down with willow bark and cold mud. When she woke, he was still there. He did not smile. He just said, "You owe me."

She has been following him ever since.

Her mismatched eyes give her strange vision: she sees depth differently than other wolves, which is why she trips so often. But she also sees things others miss: the way light bends through swamp mist, the tiny movements of insects that reveal hidden paths. Mirewort calls it "the Maw's second sight." Soot is not sure she believes that, but she keeps the compliment close to her heart.

## Skills

**Herblore (Intelligence +4):** Learning quickly; knows basics of rot-lung and wound treatment.

**Perception (Wisdom +3):** Her strange vision catches details others miss.

**Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Small and quiet; can slip through reeds without a sound.

## Weaknesses

**Clumsy (Dexterity -3):** Trips constantly. Cannot be trusted with sharp objects. Stealth exempt.

**Insecure (Charisma -2):** Needs constant reassurance. Will freeze if scolded harshly.

**Overly Attached to Mirewort (Wisdom -2):** Would follow him into danger without question. Herblore and medicine exempt.

## RP Sample

Soot balanced the gourd on her nose, trying to pour the murky liquid into a smaller container. Her mismatched eyes crossed with concentration. The gourd wobbled.

"Don't spill it," Mirewort said from behind her.

She spilled it.

"Sorry! I'm sorry. I'll get more—" She scrambled for the herb pile, knocked over three more containers, and landed face-first in a patch of stinging nettle.

Mirewort sighed. But he did not yell. He never yelled. He just walked over, helped her up, and started picking nettles out of her fur.

"You're hopeless, you little vole-snout," he said.

"I know," she mumbled.

"Good. Hopeless wolves live longer. They don't expect to succeed." He said.

She blinked at him. Was that… encouragement? She could not tell. But he did not send her away. That was enough.
