# Firepaw

**Name:** Firepaw  
**Pack:** Thistlehide  
**Rank:** Medic Apprentice  
**Age:** Adult (~3 years)  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** She-wolf (she/her)

## Appearance

A thick coat of fiery orange fur mixed with deep brown and rusty-red streaks, giving her a warm, autumn-like look. She has a strong, slim build with broad shoulders, sharp claws, and bright amber eyes clouded milky-white; blind since puphood. Lighter markings ring her paws and back. She wears an adornment of pale bones laced along her torso like a ribcage, curving from shoulder to flank over the rust-red fur, with a few more along one shoulder; fierce and deliberate, not hidden in her coat.

## Personality

Sharp, defensive, and quick to snap when wolves treat her blindness as weakness. Her tone can be harsh or impatient, but it protects pride more than it expresses true anger. With the injured she becomes calm, gentle, and focused; patience and quiet compassion she rarely shows elsewhere.

Despite blindness she is among the most capable apprentices in the den. Hearing is razor-sharp: she catches leaf-shift, snow-footsteps, and changes in breathing from prey and allies alike. She maps unfamiliar ground through scent, sound, and memory, moving as if she can see it. In healing, touch and smell make her precise with herbs and dressings; hardship sharpened her into something exceptional.

## Backstory

Born in Thistlehide without sight. Elders whispered she would never hunt or serve the den. Rejection hardened her pride instead of breaking it. She apprenticed under Sypha, the Fur-Giver, learning plants by texture and wound-work by touch when others demanded eyes she does not have.

She is still a Medic Apprentice; the pack tests her temper as often as her skill. She refuses pity and sometimes takes reckless risks to prove she belongs at the healer's shelves.

## Skills

**Touch Herblore (Herblore +4):** Identifies Thistlehide herbs by scent and texture; rarely mis-picks under pressure.

**Acute Hearing (Tracking +4, Medicine +4):** Tracks movement in brush and snow; reads pain and fear in breathing.

**Gentle Hands (Medicine +3, +1 HP on treat):** Steady, gentle hands when treating; dressings placed by touch hold clean.

## Weaknesses

**Total Blindness (Wisdom -3):** Cannot hunt by sight or read facial tells; disadvantage on most sight-linked checks; hunting payout ×0.45. Medicine, herblore, and stealth exempt.

**Defensive Temper (Charisma -3):** Snaps and snarls when doubted; reacts before thinking in tense moments.

**Refuses Help (Wisdom -2):** Struggles to trust others; teamwork frays when she will not accept aid. Medicine and herblore exempt.

**Pride Before Proof (Strength/Hunting/Survival -2):** Takes unnecessary risks to prove blindness is not weakness; falters in chaos she cannot map fast enough.

## RP Sample

The den was dark and filled with tension. Firepaw listened as Sypha tended a wolf injured on patrol, handing dried herbs when asked, nose finding bundles too far for her mentor to reach.

"Would this work?" she asked quietly, setting cobweb and yarrow beside Sypha's paws.

"Yes, perfect. Thank you."

Firepaw nosed the ground where she had been told to find feverfew, tail twitching with nerves until Sypha's approving howl made it wag despite herself. She smoothed her expression; she was not used to pride, and she did not mean to distract her mentor.

"Good. You know your herbs and where to find them. Now learn how to use them."

Firepaw's ears perked. She nodded, eager for the next lesson.

Later she insisted on helping with another casualty, trying to prove her worth. Sypha tsked and turned back to the patient. "Please," Firepaw begged. "I know I can do this. My blindness means nothing." She started forward; Sypha blocked her. Anger bubbled; she snarled and turned away with the herbs still in her jaws.

Then a new wolf stumbled in, whining in pain. Firepaw set her temper aside and guided him to a quiet nook. She lifted the injured paw, smelled warmth over the torn pad, and chose dressings from the shelves by scent alone. Sypha's presence hovered at her shoulder; she ignored it until the work was done.

"Be careful. Do not push yourself past water and rest; let yourself heal."

Firepaw flinched, ears dropping, expecting punishment. Sypha inspected the dressing and hummed approval. "You did good. Maybe you can help after all."
