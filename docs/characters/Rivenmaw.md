# Rivenmaw

**Name:** Rivenmaw  
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Hunter (River Patrol)  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

## Appearance

A medium-built grey wolf with a coat the color of a storm cloud, darkening to charcoal along his spine and muzzle, with pale silver fur on his chest and belly like river foam. His fur is short and water-slick, often damp at the paws from constant work along the riverbanks. He has striking green eyes, the same sharp hue as Mossheart's, though his gaze is steady where the Thistlehide scout's flinches. A crescent of old bite scars rings his right foreleg, a memento from a pike fight he lost as an apprentice. He moves with loose-limbed confidence on bank and stone, unhurried unless the current turns.

## Personality

Dry-humored, loyal, outwardly unflappable, privately reckless.

Rivenmaw is a wolf of few words in the den and many at the border, where news is currency and silence is suspicion. He is not cruel, but practical; Silverrush taught him that the river feeds the living, not the sentimental. That practicality makes him an excellent hunter and a reliable source of information, but it also creates a careful, calculated distance between himself and the rest of the pack. He is loyal to his duties and his packmates, but he holds a piece of himself back for the secret he keeps at the neutral bend.

With Mossheart, he softens without meaning to, becoming teasing and patient. It is the first time in seasons he has felt seen rather than merely useful. He knows their meetings could cost them both everything. He keeps coming anyway, driven by a quiet, desperate hope that outweighs his otherwise unshakeable pragmatism.

## Backstory

Born in Silverrush to a hunter mother and a Driftwood father who earned his name carrying stones until Saltmuzzle noticed his nose for fish, Rivenmaw grew up with the river in his blood. His childhood was a blend of pack duty and the freedom of the shallows, where he learned to be fast in the water and faster with the gossip from upstream. He was an average apprentice, more notable for quick wit than brawn, but skill for tracking and reading the river's moods earned him a place in the hunting corps. His father's philosophy, that a wolf's worth is measured by what they can provide, became his own.

The wound that would shape his life was not physical, but a crack in the wall of his loyalty. Three moons ago, a Thistlehide scout froze in his path at the neutral bend, bristling like a cornered thistle. Instead of posturing or aggression, Rivenmaw sat down, offered half a trout, and waited. Mossheart did not run. Over subsequent moons they met in secret, trading patrol timings and, slowly, trust. It was Mossheart who first spoke of the warming water and the strange tooth, and Rivenmaw, who had always accepted the Maw's cold judgment, began to question the foundation of his faith. The wound is now a raw, constant ache: quiet doubt every time he looks at the river.

The Maw was once as straightforward as the current. The river provides, the river takes; it is cold and impartial, and a wolf's only duty is to survive its trials. The secret meetings have chipped away at that certainty. He no longer prays for the river's favor, but for the safety of a single wolf from an enemy pack. The only human encounter of note was a childhood glimpse of a lone trapper on the far bank, pale and strange, with no place in the river's natural order. A memory that now feels like premonition.

## Skills

**Hunting (Strength +4):** Strong jaws and river sense. Brings down fish and waterfowl cleanly.

**Swimming (Dexterity +3):** Fast in shallows and eddies. Reads the current before it shifts.

**Perception (Wisdom +3):** Picks up patrol patterns and border scents before others notice.

**Herbal Knowledge (Intelligence +3):** Basic knowledge of common herbs and poultices for minor wounds and ailments.

## Weaknesses

**Secret Romance (Charisma -3):** Love for a Thistlehide wolf. Leverage in the wrong paws ends them both.

**Reckless at the Border (Wisdom -2):** Stays too long at neutral ground when Mossheart needs him to leave.

**Pack Loyalty vs Heart (Charisma -2):** If Saltmuzzle orders him to report Thistlehide movements, he will fracture.

## Maw-Belief

Doubter. He wants to believe, but the Maw's plan seems chaotic and cruel since Mossheart. He trusts his own senses and prays now only for one wolf's safety, not the river's favor.

## RP Sample

Rivenmaw waited at the neutral bend, tail curled over his paws. The Thistlehide side was late, again. The chill of the bank seeped into his bones; the river's gurgle was the only sound. When Mossheart finally slipped from the bramble, fur bristling with burrs, Rivenmaw did not scold him.

"You're late," he said, and meant it gently.

"I know. Patrol."

Rivenmaw nuzzled his cheek. "You're always hiding," he murmured, a quiet tease. Mossheart's shoulders eased a fraction.

They shared cold fish in silence, watching the sun bleed through the trees. Rivenmaw wanted to ask about the mill, the warming water, the tooth, but Mossheart was shaking. So Rivenmaw stayed quiet. He let the scout lean into him until the trembling stopped. The river burbled on, indifferent to two wolves caught between duty and something far more dangerous.
