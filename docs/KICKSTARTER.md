## 4. the story (problem, solution, why you)

### the problem
- the bot is live but fragile: when the dev machine or process stops, the den goes quiet.
- solo dev bandwidth: exploration content, pack events, and ux polish need dedicated time.
- small but committed community (~30 wolves in discord today). growth needs infrastructure before marketing can stick.

### the solution ($8,000 funds)

**24/7 hosting (~$1,200)**  
vps (railway/fly/digitalocean), backups, monitoring. 12 months.

**development time (~$5,500)**  
~220 hrs at ~$25/hr equivalent. exploration, pack systems, bugfix/polish sprints.

**kickstarter and payment fees (~$800)**  
~10% platform + processing.

**fulfillment buffer (~$500)**  
manual rewards (blurbs, commissions), backer survey tools, contingency.

### why basil / why trust
- creator of fable; bot is playable now (not vaporware).
- existing ko-fi for transparent ongoing support and shop (revive, reincarnation, bone pouches).
- open roadmap; monthly backer updates during fulfillment.
- personal rewards never drain pack treasury. same rule as ko-fi.

## 11. faq

**is fable pay-to-win?**  
no. backer rewards are personal thank-yous, cosmetics, lore, and in-bot inventory items. never pack treasury drains or exclusive combat advantages. kickstarter tiers do not include store credit or vouchers.

**how is this different from ko-fi?**  
ko-fi = ongoing tips and shop. kickstarter = one-time fund for 12 months hosting + a fixed feature roadmap.

**do i need to join discord?**  
yes. all in-game rewards require `/register`.

**which great pack should i pick?**  
any. greyspire, mistmoor, thistlehide, and silverrush are designed as equals. loners are supported too.

**i'm not in your server yet. can i back?**  
yes. join discord, `/register`, then complete the backer survey with your user id.

**when do features ship?**  
hosting (milestone a) within 30 days of funding. other milestones over the following 12 months. see roadmap.

**what if the campaign fails?**  
ko-fi and current development continue; 24/7 hosting and the expanded roadmap wait for a future funding push.

**refunds?**  
per kickstarter policy. digital goods may be non-refundable after delivery. state clearly in tier descriptions.

**can packs "win" the campaign?**  
no mechanical advantage. tier 7 patron credits are flavor acknowledgments only.
