## 2. hero video script (~75 seconds)

structure is a story arc, not a feature list: **hook (a feeling) -> what it is -> why it's deep -> the honest ask -> the vision -> call to action.** open on the strongest atmospheric asset you have (wolf art, or a striking rollover/injury/death message), never a terminal or a `/register` screen. the ask lands emotionally, tied to the real $1,500 hosting goal.

**0 to 8s; the hook (a feeling, no explanation yet)**  
visual: dim, atmospheric; a lone wolf silhouette, or a real in-game message on screen: "finnpelt barely survived septic shock last sunrise. three failed death saves. the medic reached him on turn two."  
voiceover (or caption): "somewhere, a wolf is dying. whether he sees the next sunrise is not up to luck; it's up to his pack."

**8 to 20s; what it is**  
visual: a rollover announcement turning the world over; a hunt; an injury appearing on a `/profile`  
voiceover: "howlbert is a discord bot for realistic, sentient wolf roleplay. every sunrise the world turns; you hunt, you heal, you survive hunger, thirst, injury, and the seasons."

**20 to 35s; why it's deep (the differentiator)**  
visual: `/vitals` conditions, a disease penalty, a pack gathering; text flashes: "30+ diseases · real injuries · four great packs"  
voiceover: "nothing here is flavor text. a snakebite spreads. a broken jaw changes what you can eat. join greyspire, mistmoor, thistlehide, or silverrush; your pack, your legend. story over pay-to-win."

**35 to 52s; the honest ask (the emotional core)**  
visual: the den, warm and alive, then a hard cut to "bot offline"; then back to life  
voiceover: "there's one problem. right now the den only lives while my laptop is on. i'm raising **$1,500**; enough to keep howlbert awake 24/7 for a full year, so no wolf's story stops because i closed a window."

**52 to 66s; the vision (stretch goals, honestly scoped)**  
visual: simple roadmap cards: the gathering, fourtrees diplomacy, combat depth, content drop  
voiceover: "clear that, and stretch goals fund the roadmap: a cross-pack gathering at the maw's throat, real diplomacy, deeper combat and healing. the bigger dreams get their own campaign later. this one just keeps the lights on."

**66 to 75s; call to action**  
visual: ko-fi + discord + kickstarter logos; the pack, together  
voiceover: "back the den. join the discord. let's keep the wild alive; together."

**production notes for a small campaign:**  
✦ no voice actor needed; clean screen recordings plus captions read well, but the hook (first 8s) should be your single best visual, not gameplay ui  
✦ keep it to ~75s; retention drops hard past 90s on a first campaign  
✦ subtitles/captions always on (most social autoplay is muted)  
✦ the offline -> online beat is the spine of the whole video; give it the most weight

## 3. opening pitch (first 2 paragraphs, above the fold)

howlbert is a discord bot for realistic, sentient wolf rp: daily rollovers, hunting and hoarding, great pack politics, quests, exploration, combat, legacy, and a bones economy; all built so story matters more than pay-to-win.

howlbert already runs in our discord today. this kickstarter funds one year of 24/7 hosting first, then a stretch-funded development roadmap: cross-pack politics, fourtrees diplomacy, combat and healing depth, and a content drop for all four great packs (greyspire, mistmoor, thistlehide, silverrush). ko-fi supports day-to-day costs and optional shop items. the base goal is small on purpose; bigger expansions like new exploration biomes are planned as a separate future campaign. this one is about always-on uptime and a deliverable set of features on a public timeline.

## 4. the story (problem, solution, why you)

### the problem
- the bot is live but fragile: when the dev machine or process stops, the den goes quiet.
- solo dev bandwidth: exploration content, pack events, and ux polish need dedicated time.
- small but committed community (~30 wolves in discord today). growth needs infrastructure before marketing can stick.

### the solution ($1,500 base funds)

the base goal is deliberately lean: it covers only what the den *cannot* run without. because kickstarter is all-or-nothing, a low, honest floor is safer to hit and funds faster; the feature roadmap is funded by stretch goals on top (see section 7).

**24/7 hosting (~$1,200)**  
vps (railway/fly/digitalocean), backups, monitoring. 12 months. this is the campaign's core promise.

**kickstarter and payment fees (~$150)**  
~10% platform + processing at the base goal.

**fulfillment buffer (~$150)**  
manual rewards (blurbs), backer survey tools, contingency.

**development time (funded by stretch goals)**  
the feature milestones (b to e) are roughly ~220 hrs of work. at the base goal that time is donated sweat equity and progresses as bandwidth allows; each stretch goal converts it into funded, scheduled sprints. bigger expansions beyond this roadmap are planned as a separate future kickstarter rather than stretched onto this one.

### why basil / why trust
- creator of howlbert; bot is playable now (not vaporware).
- existing ko-fi for transparent ongoing support and shop (revive, reincarnation, bone pouches).
- open roadmap; monthly backer updates during fulfillment.
- personal rewards never drain pack treasury. same rule as ko-fi.

## 6. deliverables roadmap (what backers fund)

present as a timeline graphic. all packs benefit equally.

**how the roadmap is funded:** milestone a is guaranteed by the base $1,500 goal. milestones b to e are the development roadmap; each is unlocked by a stretch goal (section 7). if a stretch isn't reached, that milestone still moves forward as donated time allows, just without a paid schedule.

### milestone a; launch + 30 days (funded immediately by the base goal)
- [ ] migrate bot to 24/7 vps with auto-restart and basic monitoring
- [ ] backer credits in `README.md` and `/patron`
- [ ] kickstarter backer badge on active wolf's `/profile` footer (permanent cosmetic)

### milestone b; months 2 to 4: the gathering at the maw's throat
a full cross-pack political gathering system. the maw's throat is a neutral volcanic caldera where all four great packs meet. alphas speak from the tongue-stone; the bone-draw decides speaking order; audiences react in real time.

- [ ] `/gather action:call` for alphas; acceptance window until next rollover (≥2 other alphas required to proceed)
- [ ] attend, speak, and advance commands; bone-draw speaking order weighted by pack founding date with gravelback npc grudge modifier
- [ ] `/gather action:react type:growl|stamp|whine` for all attendees; pack relations update immediately on reaction
- [ ] offering omen fires at close (straight smoke = flavor; twisted smoke = −1 pack relations between all attending packs); twilight zone active until next rollover
- [ ] speech history stored permanently; `/gather action:history` retrieves all past speeches
- [ ] non-attendance penalty: −2 standing per member of packs who accepted but did not send wolves

### milestone c; months 4 to 6: fourtrees diplomacy
new political levers around fourtrees (the cat+wolf neutral gathering) and cross-pack relations.

- [ ] truce enforcement window: for 2 days around fourtrees, raids and crimes against treaty packs carry doubled standing penalty
- [ ] `/gather action:speak` for alphas and diplomats: one short announcement (≤200 chars) per gathering season; visible in all packs' gathering reports
- [ ] gathering prophecy: a flavor oracle line drawn from a rotating pool each fourtrees; the medic with the highest healing skill in each pack receives a bonus vision note
- [ ] post-gathering bones exchange: `/gather action:trade` opens cross-pack bones transfer for 1 day after close (neutral+ standing required between packs)
- [ ] fourtrees absence penalty: packs without active cat pacts lose −1 unity each gathering instead of only receiving a flavor note

### milestone d; months 6 to 9: combat and healing depth
targeted fixes for systems that currently have gaps or feel unfinished.

- [ ] bleeding rules fix: staunch permanently clears active bleeding (not a 1-turn suppression); out-of-combat deep_gash escalates to infected_wound if left untreated past sunrise without a herb save
- [ ] death save threshold enforced: 3 failed saves = wolf dies at next sunrise if no medic intervention; currently wolves can sit dying indefinitely
- [ ] attack injury weighting: bite rolls weight toward deep_gash and broken_jaw; claw toward torn_claw and sprained_leg; charge toward fractured_rib and concussion — injuries now match how the wolf was actually hit
- [ ] malunion re-surgery: `set_bone` extended to allow re-surgery on malunion long-term injuries (dc 22; success clears the malunion)
- [ ] swim therapy cap: capped at one-third of total confinement days per bone injury so it cannot trivially wipe a 28-day spinal confinement
- [ ] bugfix sprint from backer and discord feedback list

### milestone e; months 9 to 12: content drop
- [ ] 4 pack-flavored daily quest variants (one per great pack; same reward bands; different flavor and location)
- [ ] 8 to 12 new herbs or prey entries distributed by terrain
- [ ] speaking stones: anonymous `/gather action:mark` during gatherings; marks revealed collectively at close with no attribution
- [ ] reserve month for overflow and delayed items from milestones b to d

**if delayed:** monthly updates; priority order a, then b, then c, then d, then e.

## 7. stretch goals (after the $1,500 base is funded)

each stretch converts a roadmap milestone from donated time into a funded, scheduled sprint. they are ordered so the most-requested systems come first.

**$3,000, milestone b funded; the gathering at the maw's throat**  
the full cross-pack political gathering system on a paid schedule (months 2 to 4).

**$4,500, milestone c funded; fourtrees diplomacy**  
truce windows, gathering speeches, prophecies, and cross-pack bones exchange (months 4 to 6).

**$6,000, milestone d funded; combat and healing depth**  
bleeding rules, death-save enforcement, injury weighting, malunion re-surgery, and a bugfix sprint (months 6 to 9).

**$8,000, milestone e funded + founding landmarks**  
content drop (quests, herbs/prey, speaking stones) plus backer-voted names for 4 neutral map locations.

**stretch beyond $8,000:** a read-only public web profile viewer, and an optional 3-month part-time moderator stipend for discord rp support.

**beyond this campaign:** larger expansions; a second exploration biome, a full web app; are planned as a **separate future kickstarter** rather than piled onto this one. keeping this campaign's scope small keeps the promises deliverable; the next big leap gets its own honest goal later.

stretch goals will be announced when the base goal hits ~70%.

## 11. faq

**is howlbert pay-to-win?**  
no. backer rewards are personal thank-yous, cosmetics, lore, and in-bot inventory items. never pack treasury drains or exclusive combat advantages. kickstarter tiers do not include store credit or vouchers.

**how is this different from ko-fi?**  
ko-fi = ongoing tips and shop. kickstarter = one-time fund for 12 months hosting + a fixed feature roadmap.

**do i need to join discord?**  
yes. all in-game rewards require `/register`.

**which great pack should i pick?**  
any. greyspire, mistmoor, thistlehide, and silverrush are designed as equals. loners are supported too.

**i'm not in your server yet. can i back?**  
yes. join discord, `/register`, then complete the backer survey with your user id.

**when do features ship?**  
hosting (milestone a) within 30 days of funding. other milestones over the following 12 months. see roadmap.

**what if the campaign fails?**  
ko-fi and current development continue; 24/7 hosting and the expanded roadmap wait for a future funding push.

**refunds?**  
per kickstarter policy. digital goods may be non-refundable after delivery. state clearly in tier descriptions.

**can packs "win" the campaign?**  
no mechanical advantage. tier 7 patron credits are flavor acknowledgments only.
