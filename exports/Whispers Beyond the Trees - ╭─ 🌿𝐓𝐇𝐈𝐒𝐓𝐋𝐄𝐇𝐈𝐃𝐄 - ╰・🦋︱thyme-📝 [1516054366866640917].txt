**Appearance:**  
Thyme is a small, neat wolf with a coat of pale green-gray and soft cream — like thyme growing on a limestone wall. Zir fur is short and smooth, and ze is always immaculately clean. Zir eyes are a bright, intelligent hazel, and ze has a small, precise scar on zir upper lip (from a diplomatic mission gone wrong). Zir tail is long and expressive, often curled over zir back. Ze moves with a dancer’s grace, each step economical.

**Personality:**  
*Witty, calm, perceptive, private, fiercely principled.*  

Thyme is the youngest diplomat in Thistlehide, and already the most effective. Ze listens more than ze speaks. Ze asks questions that cut to the heart of a dispute. Ze finds compromises that leave both sides feeling heard.

Ze is intensely private. No one knows where ze sleeps or if ze has a mate. Ze deflects personal questions with a smile and a change of subject. Some wolves find zir cold. Others find zir fascinating.

**Backstory:**  
Thyme was born to a diplomat mother and a hunter father. Zir mother taught ze to read wolves — their postures, their silences, their lies. Zir father taught ze that words are weapons.

When ze was two, ze negotiated a truce between Thistlehide and Silverrush after a border fight killed three wolves. The truce lasted a full year — longer than anyone expected. Finn’pelt named ze a diplomat on the spot.

Ze has not failed a negotiation since.
