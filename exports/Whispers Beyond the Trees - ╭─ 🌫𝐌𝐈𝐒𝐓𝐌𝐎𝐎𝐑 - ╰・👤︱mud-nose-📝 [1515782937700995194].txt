# NPC

**Name:** Mudnose  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Bog-Born (general pack member)  
**Age:** 5 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Mudnose is a stocky, thick-furred wolf with a brown coat so dark it is almost black. His most distinctive feature is his nose: permanently stained dark brown from years of digging for roots and tubers. His eyes are small and dark, nearly invisible in his broad face. He has a thick, bushy tail that he uses to slap away biting flies. His build is squat and powerful, built for rooting rather than running.

**Personality:**  

Mudnose is the wolf every pack needs, but no one notices. He digs. He forages. He patches dens. He never complains, never volunteers, never seeks attention. He is simply *there*, a fixture of the swamp like the cypress knees and the hanging moss.

But he watches. He listens. He knows where the bodies are buried, literally. He is the one who digs the graves for wolves who die of rot-lung, and he whispers their names into the soil before covering them. He remembers everyone. He is the pack’s living memory, though no one knows it.


[6/14/2026 2:23 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Mudnose was born in Mistmoor and has never left. His parents were Bog-Born, his grandparents were Bog-Born, back to the Sundering. He has never wanted to be anything else. He is content with mud and roots and the slow, patient work of keeping the dens from collapsing.

His wound is small but deep: when he was a pup, he found a Drown-Sick dying in a sinkhole. The Drown-Sick grabbed him with the last of her strength and whispered something in his ear: secret, a name, a prophecy. Mudnose has never told anyone what she said. But sometimes, when the moon is right, he goes to that sinkhole and whispers back.

**Skills:**

- **Survival (Wisdom +4):** Expert digger and den-builder.
- **Foraging (Intelligence +3):** Knows every edible root and tuber in the swamp.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Can hide in plain sight by standing completely still.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Slow Runner (Dexterity -3):** Cannot escape a predator. Relies on hiding.
- **Grumpy (Charisma -2):** Drives away wolves who might otherwise be allies.
- **Secretive (Wisdom -2):** His secrets could be dangerous if revealed.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox — but a private, unspoken orthodoxy. He does not pray aloud. He prays with his paws, digging, burying, remembering.

**RP Sample:**  
Mudnose dug. The soil was soft today, wet from last night’s rain. He pushed his broad muzzle into the earth, pulled out a root, and added it to the pile beside him. It was not exciting work. It was not glorious. But the pack would eat.

A shadow fell over him. He looked up. Mirewort stood there, watching.

“You dug up the grave,” Mirewort said. It was not a question.

Mudnose looked at the hole. It was, indeed, the spot where he had buried Sedgepup, the pup who died of shaking-sickness. He had not meant to dig there. But his paws had remembered.

“She was medicine,” Mudnose said. “You said that.”

Mirewort was silent for a long moment. Then he sat down beside the hole.

“Yes,” he said. “She was.”

They sat together in the mud, two quiet wolves, while the swamp breathed around them. Mudnose scraped a pawful of earth over the exposed root and muttered, *“Rest easy, little one.”*

**Other:** Open to plots where his secret (the Drown-Sick’s whisper) is revealed, or where he is forced to leave Mistmoor for the first time.
