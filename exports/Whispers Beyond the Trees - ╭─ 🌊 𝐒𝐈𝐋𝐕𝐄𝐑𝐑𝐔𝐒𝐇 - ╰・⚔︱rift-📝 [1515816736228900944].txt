# NPC

**Name:** Rift  
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Beta
**Age:** 6 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Rift is a long, lean wolf with a coat of pale silver-gray and white:  like moonlight on shallow water. His fur is thin and often wet, plastered to his muscular frame. His eyes are a pale, icy blue, and he has a deep, jagged scar across his chest (from a Greyspire raid). His tail is unusually short: bitten off by a pike as a pup. He moves with a quiet, liquid grace, but when he stands still, he is utterly motionless, like a stone in the current.

**Personality:**  

Rift is Saltmuzzle’s shadow. He does not speak much, but when he does, other wolves listen. He is the one who enforces her orders, who breaks up fights, who decides which border disputes are worth a rend. He is not cruel, he is *efficient*. Wolves respect him. Few like him.

He has been in love with Saltmuzzle for years. He has never told her. He knows she still grieves her dead mate. So he waits. He watches. He keeps her safe. That is enough.

**Backstory:**  
Rift was born in Silverrush to a hunter and a scout. His mother drowned when he was a pup; swept away by the current. He learned to swim in that same current, refusing to let the river take him too. He became a guard, then Saltmuzzle’s second, by being the wolf who never drowned.

He carries the guilt of not being able to save Saltmuzzle’s mate. He was there when the Greyspire raiders attacked. He was too slow. He has been trying to make up for it ever since.

**Skills:**
- **Swimming (Dexterity +4):** Strong, fast, can cross the river in a quarter of the time it takes others.
- **Fighting (Strength +3):** Prefers to fight in water, where his reach and leverage are best.
- **Perception (Wisdom +3):** Notices disturbances in the current — a sign of danger upstream.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Silent (Charisma -3):** Wolves often forget he is there. He misses social cues.
- **Guilt-Ridden (Wisdom -3):** Will take unnecessary risks to protect Saltmuzzle.
- **Secret Love (Charisma-2):** Could be exploited by an enemy who discovers it.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox — the river is the Maw’s tears. He swims to feel close to her.

**RP Sample:**  
Rift stood at the river’s edge, his pale blue eyes fixed on the far bank. Saltmuzzle was behind him, speaking to a scout. He did not turn. He did not need to. He knew her voice, her scent, the rhythm of her breathing.

“Rift.” Her voice. Soft. Tired.

“Current.”

“The Greyspire patrol is moving west. Take a team. Watch them. Do not engage.”

He nodded. He had already chosen the wolves. He had already scouted the route. He was always three steps ahead, that was his job.

He turned to leave. Saltmuzzle touched his shoulder with her nose.

“Be careful.”

He did not answer. He could not promise that.

**Other:** Open to plots where his feelings for Saltmuzzle are revealed, or where his guilt drives him to sacrifice himself.
