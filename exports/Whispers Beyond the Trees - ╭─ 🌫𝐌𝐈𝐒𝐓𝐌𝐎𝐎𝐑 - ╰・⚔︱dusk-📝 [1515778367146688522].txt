# NPC

**Name:** Dusk
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Second (Whisper-Jaw)  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Bisexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Dusk is a solid, muscular wolf with a dark gray coat that fades to black on his legs and muzzle. His eyes are a warm, almost golden brown: the only warm thing about him. He has a distinctive scar across his throat, a wound that should have killed him (it came from a Greyspire raid, and he still cannot howl properly; his voice is a rasping whisper). His ears are heavily notched, and his teeth are surprisingly white for a Mistmoor wolf; he cleans them obsessively with abrasive reeds.

**Personality:**  

Dusk is the enforcer of Murkvein’s will. He does not like the role, he would rather be a hunter, a simple wolf with simple duties, but he is good at it. He watches wolves. He reports on wolves. He executes wolves. And he hates himself a little more each time.

He is fiercely protective of the pack’s pups, who do not fear him the way adults do. He tells them stories in his rasping whisper, voices all the characters, makes them laugh. No one else sees this side of him. He prefers it that way.

**Backstory:**  
Dusk was born to a Bog-Born mother and an unknown father. He was a large, quiet pup who did not speak until he was nearly a year old: the other pups thought he was mute. He was not. He was *listening*. When he finally spoke, his voice was a rasp, scarred by a throat infection he had hidden for moons.

**Skills:**  
Intimidation (Charisma +4): His rasping whisper and scarred throat make him sound like a ghost.

Stealth (Dexterity +3): Moves through the swamp like smoke.

Tracking (Intelligence +3): Can follow a trail through standing water.

**Weaknesses:**  
Rasping Voice (Charisma -2)
Cannot howl. Cannot call for help. His throat is a ruin of scar tissue from a gator's jaws in his youth. He relies on others for long-distance communication, and in emergencies, he is left silent while others raise the alarm.

**Applies to:** Any Charisma check involving vocal communication, intimidation through speech, persuasion, or rallying others. He cannot participate in pack howls or long-distance calls at all.


Loyal to a Fault (Wisdom -3)
He would follow his alpha into madness, disease, or death itself. This unyielding loyalty has led him into traps, ambushes, and morally compromised situations. He cannot say no to a direct order, even when every instinct screams otherwise.

**Applies to:** Wisdom checks to resist, question, or delay following a direct order from his alpha or other recognized superiors. Also applies to resisting manipulation by those superiors.

Guilt (Wisdom -2)
He remembers every wolf he has executed. They visit him in dreams, not as monsters, but as they were in life. The weight of memory slows his paw, clouds his judgment, and makes him hesitate in ways that would have saved him had he acted without thought.

**Applies to:** Wisdom checks involving concentration, clear decision-making, and resisting emotional influence when confronted with reminders of his executions: the kin of the dead, the scent of blood, the cry of a wolf in pain.
