**Gender/Pronouns:** Nonbinary (xe/xir)

**Appearance:**  
Stonepiercer is a lean, angular wolf with a coat of mottled gray and white — like fractured granite. Xir fur is short and rough, often dusted with rock powder from xir climbs. Xir eyes are a pale, piercing yellow, and xe has a thin scar running from xir left ear to xir jaw (a fall onto ice). Xir tail is unusually long and bushy, used for balance on narrow ledges. Xe moves with a jerky, precise gait, each paw placed with intention.

**Personality:**  
*Observant, laconic, competitive, fair, secretly sentimental.*  

Stonepiercer speaks little, but when xe does, xir words are sharp and exact. Xe prefers the company of mountains to wolves. Other Greyspire wolves find xe unsettling — not because xe is cruel, but because xe is *still*. Xe can sit on a ridge for an entire day, watching, waiting, not moving a muscle.

Xe is fiercely competitive with the other scouts, though xe never brags. Xe simply brings back more information, faster, and lets the results speak. Xe has a secret: xe keeps a small pouch of smooth stones collected from every successful patrol. Xe touches them before sleep, counting xir achievements.

**Backstory:**  
Stonepiercer was born a Lowbelly, the runt of a litter of five. Xir littermates died of cold during a harsh leaf-bare. Xe survived by crawling into a crevice and sleeping against a warm vent. The pack found xe a moon later, half-starved but alive.

Xe became a scout because xe could not fight. Xe learned to read the mountain — the way shadows fell, the shift of loose scree, the smell of coming storms. When xe was two, xe discovered a hidden pass that allowed Greyspire to flank a Silverrush patrol. The Highfang at the time (Grim’s predecessor) named xe Stonepiercer. Xe has worn the name like armor ever since.
