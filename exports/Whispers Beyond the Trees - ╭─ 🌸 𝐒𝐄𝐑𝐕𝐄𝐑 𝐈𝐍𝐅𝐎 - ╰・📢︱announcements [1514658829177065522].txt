[6/26/2026 11:58 AM] lizardpelt
@everyone eeeeeeee i just added some major features to howlbert :333

i had the idea to make it our "in-game RP" because of the stuff happening with whispering wilds >.<

this is all completely optional btw

tupperbox is great for *talking* as your wolf, but it doesn't know you're in greyspire, whether you're starving, who your mate is, or that you just earned blooding. howlbert does. one character for slash commands, hunts, bonds, injuries, pack life, and typed lines; instead of  bot + a proxy + a notes doc.

✦ proxy works tupperbox-style, but wired to your real wolf (name, avatar, pack, profile, journal)
✦ game state shows up where RP matters; mood, bonds, blooding, den news, deaths, and pack moves log automatically in `/journal`
✦ scenes, locations, ceremonies, and body language are built for this den, not generic plural tooling

more below :3 `/help topic:roleplay`for the full guide

**speak as your wolf**

✦ `/proxy set tag:H:text` then type `H:your line` 
✦ `/proxy avatar` upload and crop a portrait; `/proxy autoproxy` to default one wolf (`\` at the start of a line sends as you once)
✦ `((ooc))` or `//` in proxy text; ic posts as the wolf, ooc in the footer
✦ `/say` and `/whisper` for quick ic lines and private ic dms
✦ `/character` for pronouns, bio, birthday, avatar, ref on `/profile`

**migrating from tupperbox**

1. send `tul!export` in #╰・🤖︱bot-commands 
2. save the `.json` file it sends you
3. in this server run `/proxy import` and attach that file
4. export names must match your howlbert wolf names (`/wolves` to check). if something didn't link, `/rename` to match, then `/proxy import` again
5. optional: `/proxy avatar` if you want cropped portraits here; `/proxy autoproxy` if you had autoproxy on before


[6/26/2026 11:59 AM] lizardpelt
**body language** (not just text!)

✦ `/sign` visual signals: **alert · rally · play · submit · soothe · threaten · read**
✦ mute wolves can't `/howl`; their **rally** sign builds extra unity instead, and denmates answer with `/sign signal:read`
✦ good for tense scenes, submission, play bows, "something's wrong" without another dialogue paragraph

**scenes & presence**

✦ `/scene start` opens an rp thread with a pinned living roster; posting auto-joins you; `/scene poke` pings everyone there
✦ `/location set` for where your wolf is ic (shows on `/profile` and proxy footers)
✦ `/roster` gallery of living wolves registered in the server


**den life**

✦ `/journal` automatic timeline (register, birth, pack, bonds, blooding, death, rites); you don't write it yourself
✦ `/rite naming` · `blooding` · `mourning` ceremonies in-channel
✦ `/family` relationship tree diagram
✦ `/npc` staff register npcs; anyone can `/npc say`
