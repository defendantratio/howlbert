     **Act I**     
The Gathering at FourTrees started off normal, each leader gathered in the clearing. 
But something shifted in the air when each leader returned to their clan... all but one.
ThunderClan's leader mysteriously ||died|| during the Gathering and the other leaders refuse to say why or how.
Tension rises among the four clans as blame shifts between each leader.
ThunderClan is struggling to get their footing as the young deputy struggles to adjust to the increase in responsibility.

**What We're Looking For**
We're looking for people who have read the warrior cats books or have an understanding of canon Warrior Cats
We're aiming for a lore-accurate long-term roleplay so we can do things including but not limited to;
Gathering at FourTrees
Making the journey to the MoonStone
Border patrols, hunting parties, and herb gathering
Apprentice Training
Apprentice and Warrior Ceremonies
Minimum of 6 per clan(major roles and 3 warriors) as we don't want everyone to cluster together into one clan. Please don't switch clans mid RP.
We need 1 volunteer from each clan to take bullet notes on major events that take place.
