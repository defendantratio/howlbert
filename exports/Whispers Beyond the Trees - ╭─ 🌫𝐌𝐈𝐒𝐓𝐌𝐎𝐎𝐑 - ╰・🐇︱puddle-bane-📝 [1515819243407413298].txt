# NPC

**Name:** Puddlebane  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Forager  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Puddlebane is a squat, thick-bodied wolf with a coat of dark brown and greenish-black: the color of a rain-soaked log. Her fur is greasy and matted, and she is always dripping with swamp water. Her eyes are a pale, watery yellow, and she has a large, bulbous nose that twitches constantly. Her paws are wide and splayed, perfect for walking on soft mud without sinking. Her tail is short and stubby (a birth defect). She smells of rotting vegetation and stagnant water.

**Personality:**  

Puddlebane is the only wolf in Mistmoor who smiles. She smiles at everyone — even wolves who growl at her. She is not naive; she simply refuses to let the swamp make her miserable. She finds joy in rotting logs, glowing fungus, and the way the mist curls through the cypress knees.

She is absent-minded, often forgetting where she left her herb pouch or which direction is the den. But when it comes to plants, edible, medicinal, poisonous, she remembers everything. She can smell a death-cap mushroom from a tree-length away.


[6/14/2026 4:46 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Puddlebane was born in Mistmoor to a Bog-Born mother and an unknown father. She was a sickly pup, prone to rot-lung and fevers. A healer saved her life with a poultice made from a rare fungus that grows only on drowned logs. She has been obsessed with plants ever since.

She has no mate, no pups. Her mother died two years ago. She lives alone in a hollow cypress, surrounded by drying herbs and rotting mushrooms. She is content.

**Skills:**
- **Foraging (Intelligence +3):** Expert in swamp plants; edible roots, medicinal fungi, poisonous berries.
- **Herblore (Intelligence +4):** Can identify any plant by smell alone.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Moves quietly through thick undergrowth.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Absent-Minded (Wisdom -2):** Forgets pack meetings, border patrols, where she left her herb pouch.
- **Cheerful (Charisma -2):** Other wolves find her unsettling. She does not fit Mistmoor's grim mood.
- **Physically Weak (Strength -3):** Cannot fight. Will not fight. Will offer a mushroom instead.

**Statistics (Total: 17 — Forager range 15–20)**  
Strength: 2 (–2)  
Dexterity: 4 (–1)  
Survival: 5 (0)  
Intelligence: 6 (+1)  
Charisma: 3 (–2)  
Wisdom: 4 (–1)

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox — the swamp is the Maw’s belly. Rot is not death; it is digestion. She thanks the Maw by leaving a piece of every plant she finds at the Belly-Rip’s edge.

**RP Sample:**  
Puddlebane waded through the shallows, her wide paws squelching in the mud. Ahead of her, a cluster of violet mushrooms glowed in the shade of a fallen cypress. She smiled.

“Hello, darlings,” she murmured. “You are beautiful today.”

She knelt and sniffed them. Edible. She pulled three, leaving the rest to spore. She tucked them into her gourd and waded on.

A shadow fell over her. She looked up. Dusk stood on a log, his golden-brown eyes flat.

“Rotmother wants you,” he said.

“Tell her I’ll be there when I finish finding the feverfew.” She smiled at him. He did not smile back.

“Now.”

She sighed. “Fine. But you’re carrying my gourd.”

She dropped the heavy container at his paws. He stared at it. She walked away, still smiling.

**Other:** Open to plots where her cheerfulness is tested by tragedy, or where she discovers a new, dangerous plant.
