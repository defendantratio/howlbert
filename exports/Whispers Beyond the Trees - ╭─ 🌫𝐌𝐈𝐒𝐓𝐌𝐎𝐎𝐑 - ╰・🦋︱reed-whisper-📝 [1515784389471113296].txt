# NPC

**Name:** Reedwhisper  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Diplomat  
**Age:** 5 years  
**Sexuality:** Bisexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Reedwhisper is a slender, almost delicate wolf with a coat of pale tan and soft greenish-gray: perfect camouflage among dead cattails and rotting logs. Her fur is short and water-repellent, and she smells faintly of mint (she chews the leaves to mask the swamp's stench). Her eyes are a warm, honey-brown: disarming, gentle. She has no visible scars, but her left ear droops slightly (nerve damage from a childhood illness). She moves with a fluid, sinuous grace that reminds other wolves of the swamp itself.


[6/14/2026 2:28 PM] lizardpelt
**Personality:**  
*Gentle, perceptive, evasive, deeply loyal to Mistmoor, unexpectedly fierce.*  

Reedwhisper is the face Mistmoor shows to the world: calm, reasonable, almost sweet. She listens more than she speaks, nods at the right moments, and never raises her voice. Other diplomats underestimate her. That is her greatest weapon.

She believes in the Maw with her whole heart: not the cruel, hungry Maw of Greyspire's stories, but the *patient* Maw, the one that digests slowly, that turns death into new life. She wants the other packs to understand that Mistmoor is not evil. It is simply *different*.

But she has a temper. It takes a lot to provoke it: betrayal, cruelty to pups, desecration of the dead. When it surfaces, her voice drops to a whisper, and her honey-brown eyes go flat. Wolves who have seen it do not forget.

**Backstory:**  
Reedwhisper was born to a Bog-Born family in Mistmoor, the only survivor of a litter of five. Her mother called her "Reed" because she was thin and flexible, bending without breaking. She grew up watching the other packs fear and hate Mistmoor. She decided, as a pup, that she would change that.

She volunteered to be the pack's diplomat at three: the youngest in Mistmoor's history. Her first mission was to Thistlehide, to negotiate a border dispute over a shared berry patch. She succeeded by bringing a gift: a bundle of feverfew, wrapped in moss, with a note (in wolf scratches) that said, *"For your healers."* Thistlehide remembered.

**Skills:**
- **Persuasion (Charisma +3):** Her gentle voice disarms hostility.
- **Perception (Wisdom +3):** Reads emotions like a tracker reads prints.
- **Herblore (Intelligence +2):** Uses gifts of medicine to sweeten negotiations.

**RP Sample:**  
Reedwhisper sat on a fallen log, her honey-brown eyes fixed on the Greyspire envoy. The mountain wolf,  a scarred male named Flint, had been shouting for the past five minutes about Mistmoor's "poison water" and "corrupt magic."


[6/14/2026 2:30 PM] lizardpelt
She let him finish. Then she smiled.

"You are afraid," she said softly. "That is why you shout, you *oak-head*."

Flint's jaws snapped shut.

"The water is not poisoned. It is *different*: as different as your mountain air is from our mist. But you do not accuse the mountain of poisoning the sky." She tilted her head. "Perhaps the problem is not the water. Perhaps the problem is that you do not understand it."

Flint had no answer. Reedwhisper waited. The silence stretched.

"Let us try again," she said. "What does Greyspire actually need?"

**Other:** Open to plots where her temper is exposed, or where she must choose between her pack's secrets and a greater peace.
