## Who We Are 🌲

Welcome! To The Cozy Camp! Have you ever felt that in a roleplay you had felt left out, didn’t understand the lore, the roleplay just *didn’t stick* or the server just died too fast before the server could really hit it off? Unfortunately this is something we’ve all been through, and with the chat bans, many of us have found this as an arising issue. So, we’ve decided to change that!


## Why Short Terms? 🏔️

With short terms, you don’t need to worry about getting bored from a story whilst everyone has an equal chance to getting a major role once every 2 weeks! With a new story from our Roleplay Coordinators, you can ensure that each plot will be completely different, and just as exciting!

We are also a Roleplay hub for partnering with wcue Roleplay servers! DM us if you’re interested in partnering!

## What We Offer! ⛄️

- weekly WCUE roleplays with rotating plots
- A welcoming 17+ community
- Competitions & game nights
- Roleplay server partnership channels & hub
- Equal opportunities for major roles
- A Cozy, low-stress environment

## Join Us! 🛷

Come rest your paws from the snow, your hospitality is more than welcome. Our Cozy Camp is where stories thrive and everyone belongs
