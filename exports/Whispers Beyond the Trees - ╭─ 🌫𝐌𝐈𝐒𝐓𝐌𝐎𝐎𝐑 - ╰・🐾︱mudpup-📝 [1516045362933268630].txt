# NPC

**Name:** Mudpup  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Pup  
**Age:** 4 moons  
**Sexuality:** Too young  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Mudpup is a solid, thick-bodied pup with a coat of dark brown and greenish-black — the exact color of wet peat. His fur is always caked with mud, no matter how much his mother grooms him. His eyes are a pale, watery yellow, and he has a distinct white patch on his chest shaped like a paw print. His paws are wide and splayed, perfect for walking on soft ground. He smells of earth and fungus.

**Personality:**  
*Quiet, observant, stubborn, surprisingly gentle, deeply superstitious.*  

Mudpup does not speak much. When he does, it is in a low, mumbling murmur that other pups find creepy. He spends most of his time sitting at the edge of the nursery den, watching the mist roll through the cypress knees. He is not lonely — he is *listening*. He hears things other wolves do not: the whisper of the Belly-Rip, the breathing of the swamp, the faint chewing sound that comes from nowhere.

He is deeply superstitious, even for a Mistmoor wolf. He leaves offerings of rotten meat at the Belly-Rip’s edge. He refuses to step on certain patterns of moss. He will not sleep in the dark without a piece of glowing fungus beside him.

**Backstory:**  
Mudpup was born in Mistmoor to a Bog-Born mother and a father he has never met. His mother is kind but distracted, often wandering into the swamp for days at a time. Mudpup has learned to be self-sufficient.

When he was two moons old, he wandered too close to the Belly-Rip and fell in. He was pulled out by a Drown-Sick before he could sink. He does not remember the fall, but he remembers the *chewing*. It was not scary. It was *friendly*. He has been different ever since.

**Skills:**
- **Stealth (Dexterity +4):** Can disappear into the swamp like smoke.
- **Perception (Wisdom +3):** Hears things other wolves miss including the Belly-Rip's whispers.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Unsettling (Charisma -3):** Other pups avoid him. He is creepy without meaning to be.
- **Physically Weak (Strength -2):** Not built for fighting or hunting. Digging is his only strength.
- **Fixated on the Belly-Rip (Wisdom -2):** Will wander toward it if not watched. Dangerous.

**Statistics (Total: 12 — Pup range 10–14)**  
Strength: 2 (–2)  
Dexterity: 3 (–2)  
Survival: 4 (–1)  
Intelligence: 2 (–2)  
Charisma: 1 (–3)  
Wisdom: 5 (0)

**Maw-Belief:** Zealot (he believes the Belly-Rip is the Maw’s mouth and it *likes* him).

**RP Sample:**  
Mudpup sat at the edge of the Belly-Rip, his wide paws dangling over the dark water. The chewing was loud today — a wet, rhythmic grinding that made his gums ache. He did not flinch. He closed his eyes and listened.

“Mudpup!” His mother’s voice, sharp with fear. “Get back from there!”

He did not move. “It’s hungry,” he said.

“I don’t care. Get back.”

He sighed and stood, turning away from the sinkhole. He walked back to the den, his paws squelching in the mud, and curled up in his nest. The chewing followed him into his dreams.

**Other:**  
- **Family:** Mother — Bog-Born named Fern. Father unknown.  
- **Open plots:** Possible Drown-Sick transformation if he falls again; his superstitions could be exploited; he might become an oracle.
