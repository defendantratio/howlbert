# NPC

**Name:** Cinder  
**Pack:** Silverrush (Driftwood — outsider accepted into the pack)  
**Rank:** Driftwood
**Age:** 2 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Cinder is a dark, ash-gray wolf with a coat that is patchy and scarred: the result of a fire that killed his birth pack. His fur is short and bristly, and it smells faintly of smoke, even now. His eyes are a bright, startling orange-yellow, like embers. One of his ears is missing entirely (burned off), and his tail is a stump; the rest lost to the same fire. He moves with a jittery, nervous energy, as if he expects the world to catch flame at any moment.


[6/15/2026 6:15 AM] lizardpelt
**Personality:**  
*Survivor, guilty, quiet, desperate to belong, secretly hopeful.*  

Cinder is the only survivor of a pack that burned to death in a wildfire — a fire caused by human carelessness (a campfire left unattended). He wandered for months, alone, before Silverrush found him on the riverbank, half-starved and half-mad with grief. They took him in. They gave him stones to carry. He carries them still.

He does not speak much. When he does, his voice is hoarse and cracked, damaged by smoke inhalation. He flinches at sudden noises — a snapping twig, a raised voice, the crackle of dry leaves. But he is gentle. He is kind. He shares his food. He grooms the pups. He wants to be good.

**Backstory:**  
Cinder was born in a small, unnamed pack in the far north: a pack that did not believe in the Maw, that followed no god but the hunt. They were happy. They were safe. Then the humans came.

He does not remember the fire clearly. He remembers heat, screaming, the smell of burning fur. He remembers running and running until his paws bled. When he stopped, he was alone. He never went back.

He found Silverrush by accident: he collapsed on their riverbank, and Ripple found him. He has been carrying stones ever since. It is the first time in his life he has felt useful.

**Maw-Belief:** Convert — he did not believe before Silverrush. Now he believes because the river saved him. That is enough.

**RP Sample:**  
Cinder carried the stone. It was heavy, a smooth river rock, the size of his head and it pressed against his chest as he walked from one bank to the other. He had done this a hundred times. A thousand. He would do it a thousand more.

A shadow fell over him. He looked up. Aromis stood on the bank, watching.

“You’re good at that,” he said.

He did not answer. He never knew what to say.

“Do you ever wonder why?” he asked. “Why we make Driftwood carry stones?”

He stopped. He looked at the rock. Then he looked at him.

“To remind us,” Cinder said, his voice cracked and quiet. “That we are not the heaviest thing in the world.”

Aromis stared at him for a long moment. Then he nodded and walked away.

Cinder carried the stone to the other bank. He dropped it. He picked up another. He kept carrying.

**Other:** Open to plots where he must confront his past, or where his trauma is triggered at a critical moment.
