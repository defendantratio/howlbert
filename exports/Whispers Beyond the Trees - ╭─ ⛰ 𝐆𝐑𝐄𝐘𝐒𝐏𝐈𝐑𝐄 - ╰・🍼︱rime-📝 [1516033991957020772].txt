**Name:** Rime 
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Caretaker
**Age:** 7 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Rime is a gaunt, scarred wolf with a coat of silver-gray and white — like ice on stone. Her fur is thin and patchy, revealing old battle scars. Her eyes are a pale, watery blue, and she has a missing chunk from her left ear (frostbite). Her whiskers are unusually long and white, giving her a wise, weathered look. She walks with a slight limp (old hip injury). Her voice is a low, rasping murmur.

**Personality:**  
*Patient, stern, fair, deeply kind, exhausted.*  

Rime has raised more pups than any wolf in Greyspire. She is not their mother: she is the one who watches them while their parents hunt and fight. She teaches them the old laws, the border marks, the proper way to bow to the Highfang. She does not coddle. Pups who whine go hungry. Pups who fight get cuffed. But she has never lost a pup to cold or sickness, and that is her pride.

She is tired. She has seen too many pups die in border raids, too many mothers grieve. She does not show it. A caretaker who shows weakness is a den-keeper who gets replaced.

**Backstory:**  
Rime was born in Greyspire to a Stoneguard father and a caretaker mother. She never wanted to fight. She wanted to *care for*. When her mother died of old age,  took her place. She has been watching pups for four years.

Her own litter was stillborn. She does not talk about it. She pours all her mothering into the pack’s pups and pretends it is enough.

**Skills:**
- **Medicine (Wisdom +4):** Basic — can treat minor wounds and identify common illnesses.
- **Persuasion (Charisma +3):** Calms frightened pups and breaks up fights.
- **Survival (Constitution +3):** Knows which dens stay warm in leaf-bare.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Old Injuries (Dexterity -3):** Limps, cannot run. If a predator attacks the den, she cannot defend it.
- **Emotionally Closed (Charisma -3):** Cannot show affection. Pups find her cold.
- **Haunted by Stillbirths (Wisdom -3):** Avoids pregnant wolves. Cannot bear to watch them.

**RP Sample:**  
Rime sat at the entrance of the nursery den, her pale blue eyes fixed on the pups tumbling in the snow. Cinderpup was trying to climb a rock; Harepup was taunting him from the top. Rime did not intervene. They would learn.

A yelp. Cinderpup had fallen. He scrambled up, fur dusted with snow, and glared at Harepup. Rime’s lip twitched — almost a smile.

“Get up,” she called. “Falling is not failing. Staying down is failing.”

Cinderpup growled and climbed again. Rime settled back, watching, waiting.
