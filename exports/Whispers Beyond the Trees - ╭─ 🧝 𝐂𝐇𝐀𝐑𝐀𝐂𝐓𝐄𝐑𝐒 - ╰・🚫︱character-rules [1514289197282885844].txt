[🧑] Character Rules
**1. Appearance & Realism**
- Wolves are wolves — not anime wolf-people. No unnatural colors (pink, blue, neon). Acceptable colors: gray, black, white, brown, tawny, reddish, and blends thereof.
- Scars, deformities, and missing parts are allowed and encouraged (this is a brutal world).
- Eye colors: yellow, amber, gold, brown, gray, pale blue. No red glowing eyes unless magically corrupted (mod approval).

**2. Starting Slots**
- New members begin with two (2) active character slots.
- After 30 days of activity, you may request a third slot.
- Maximum of four (4) characters per player, regardless of activity.

**3. Slot Usage Rules**
- Characters must be in different packs. No two characters in the same pack (to prevent conflict of interest and metagaming).
- Exception: If a pack has fewer than three active players, a player may temporarily run two characters in that pack with moderator approval.
- Loners count toward your slot total and require special approval.

**4. Inactive Characters**
- Characters inactive for 60+ days may be archived to free slots.
- Archived characters can be reactivated within 90 days. After that, they are considered forgotten (dead to the Maw) unless you reapply.
- Players leaving the server permanently forfeit their characters to the narrative (they may be NPC'd, killed, or exiled at moderator discretion).

**5. Name Structure**
Wolf names in this setting are generally two-part compound names (like Warrior Cats but with a darker tone). Examples of acceptable formats:
- **Adjective + Body Part:** Rivenmaw, Ironjaw, Softstep, Shallowclaw
- **Noun + Noun:** Throatmoss, Stoneguard, Weepstone, Bogwhisper, Shallow-Grave, Mourning-Tide
- **Verb + Noun:** Howlwind, Bitefrost, Driftshadow
- **Possessive + Noun:** Sorrowvein, Salt-Tongue, Bitterroot

**6. Prohibited Names**
- Canon names from popular IPs (Ghost, Nymeria, WhiteFang)
- Names referencing real-world brands or memes

**7. Name Changes**
- A wolf may earn a new name IC through notable deeds (e.g., Softpaw becomes Ironjaw after killing a bear).
- Name changes require moderator approval and an IC scene.
- No frivolous changes.

**8. Realistic Wolf Anatomy**
- Wolves are large canines — not monsters, not magical beasts (unless corrupted by specific plot events).
- Weight range: 60–180 lbs (27–82 kg) depending on pack and sex. Mistmoor wolves are smaller (60–100 lbs). Greyspire wolves are larger (120–180 lbs).
- Height: 26–32 inches at shoulder.
- No wings, no unnatural body modifications.

**9. Age Conversion**
1 wolf year ≈ 2 human years for developmental purposes (but wolves age faster).
- **Pup:** 0–12 months. Cannot hold rank, cannot fight in serious combat, must have adult guardian.
- **Young wolf:** 1–3 years. Can hold low ranks. Full physical maturity by 2 years.
- **Adult:** 3–8 years. Prime.
- **Senior:** 8–10 years. Slowing down, respected or culled depending on pack.
- **Elder:** 10+ years. Rare. Requires backstory justification and a "how they survived" explanation.

**10. Minimum Backstory Requirements**
Your character's backstory must answer:
- **Origin:** Were they born in the pack? Joined later? From where?
- **The Wound:** What loss, betrayal, or failure shaped them? (Can be emotional or physical.)
- **The Maw:** Do they believe? Why or why not? Have they ever felt the Maw's presence?
- **Humans:** Have they encountered humans? How did it go?

**11. Personality & Flaws Requirement**
- Every character must have at least **two visible flaws** (e.g., reckless, distrustful, cowardly, obsessive, volatile) that affect their decisions in RP.
- "Flawless" or "perfectly noble" characters are not allowed – this is a brutal world where survival demands sacrifice.
- Your character's personality must be consistent with their backstory (e.g., a wolf who lost their pack to betrayal should show some level of guardedness).

**12. Character Relationships & Pre-Existing Connections**
- You may establish pre-existing relationships (family, rivalry, past alliance) with other players' characters – but **both parties must consent** and document it in their sheets.
- New characters cannot automatically know or be trusted by established pack leaders without an IC reason or scene.
- Family trees must be approved by staff to prevent lore contradictions.

**13. Pack Transfer & Loyalty Rules**
- A character may leave their pack and join another, but this requires:
  - An **IC scene** showing the departure and the reasons behind it.
  - Staff approval (to ensure it fits the lore and doesn't disrupt pack dynamics).
- Characters cannot switch packs more than **once every 30 days** unless given special permission.
- Betraying a pack and joining an enemy pack carries severe IC consequences (expect hostility, exile, or death from the old pack).

**14. Pups & Breeding Rules**.
- Pups inherit a mix of both parents' traits (appearance, temperament) and must follow the age conversion rules.
- You cannot have more than **three (3) living pups** per characters at any given time.
- Pups require an adult guardian (one of the parents or a designated caretaker) until they reach 12 months.

**15. Healing & Injury Realism**
- Wounds heal realistically – a gash takes days or weeks to close; broken bones take months.
- You cannot "shake off" a serious injury. If your character receives a severe wound, you must:
  - Reflect it in your RP (limping, favoring a leg, slower reactions).
  - Wait a reasonable IC time before full recovery.
- Staff may enforce cooldowns on heavily injured characters to maintain realism.

**16. Unique Physical Traits & Mutations**
- While no unnatural colors are allowed, **minor unique traits** are permitted with mod approval:
  - Heterochromia (two different eye colors)
  - Piebald or vitiligo-like markings
  - Unusually large or small size (within realistic bounds)
- Major mutations (extra limbs, missing eyes from birth, unnatural skeletal structure, pituitary dwarfism) require extensive backstory justification and staff approval.

**17. Supernatural / Corrupted Characters**
- Any character touched by the Maw or possessing supernatural abilities **requires staff approval** before creation.
- Corrupted wolves must have clear limitations and weaknesses (e.g., vulnerability to certain rituals, progressive degeneration).
- Abilities are limited to subtle effects (visions, whispers, unnatural resilience) – no fireballs, telekinesis, or mind control.

**18. Death & Resurrection Policy**
- Character death is final unless explicitly part of a staff-run plot (e.g., the Maw resurrects them as a corrupted husk).
- You cannot "reroll" a dead character into a new character with the same name, personality, or backstory.
- If your character dies, you may create a new one immediately – but it must be a fresh character (no relation to the dead one unless approved).

**19. Activity & Participation Expectation**
- Characters are expected to participate in pack life – if your character is a ghost that never interacts, they may be archived or NPC'd.
- If you join a pack, you are expected to:
  - Attend pack meetings (when scheduled).
  - Respond to pack-wide events and threats.
  - Engage with packmates in at least one thread per week (or per your availability).
- Chronic inactivity may lead to your character being written out of the pack.
