# NPC

**Name:** Thorn  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Guard  
**Age:** 3 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Thorn is a rangy, scarred wolf with a coat the color of dried blood: a rusty red-brown that makes him stand out against the mountain’s gray. His fur is thin and patchy in places, revealing old burns from a volcanic vent he fell into as a pup. His eyes are a dark, almost black brown, and he has a nervous habit of licking his lips. His tail is kinked at the tip (broken and healed wrong). He is not large, but he is *fast*.

**Personality:**  

Thorn is not a natural Guard. He is too jumpy, too reactive, too desperate for approval. But he *wants* to be good at it. He trains twice as hard as anyone else, runs twice as many patrols, volunteers for every dangerous assignment. He hopes that if he works hard enough, no one will notice his fear.

He is afraid of everything: heights, darkness, other packs, the Maw, being alone, being forgotten. He hides it behind bluster and bravado. It works on most wolves. It does not work on Hemlock, who sees right through him.


[6/14/2026 4:04 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Thorn’s litter was thrown into a volcanic vent by a previous Highfang: a culling of “weak blood.” Thorn was the only one who survived, crawling out with burns across half his body and a terror of heat that never left him. He was raised by a Lowbelly who found him, a wolf named Cragheart who taught him that survival is its own reward.

Cragheart died last season: old age, not violence. Thorn was with him at the end. He held his foster-father’s paw and whispered, *“I’ll make you proud.”* He has been trying ever since.

**Skills:**
- **Fighting (Strength +4):** Fast, aggressive, uses his smaller size to get inside larger wolves' guard.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Surprisingly quiet for a wolf of his temperament.
- **Intimidation (Charisma +3):** His burn scars and wild eyes make him look dangerous.


**Weaknesses:**
- **Fear of Heights (Wisdom -3):** In a mountain pack. Will freeze on exposed ledges.
- **Hot-Headed (Wisdom -2):** Picks fights he cannot win. Needs someone to pull him back.
- **Insecure (Charisma -2):** Can be manipulated by any wolf who offers praise.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox — he believes the Maw saved him from the vent. He prays every night, pressing his burned fur to the cold stone.

**RP Sample:**  
Thorn stood at the border, his patchy fur bristling. The wind carried the scent of Mistmoor: rot and swamp and something *watching*. He hated the smell. He hated the swamp. He hated the way the mist curled like living things.

“Anything?” A voice behind him. Icefang.

Thorn jumped. “No. Nothing. Quiet.”

Icefang stared at him with those flat blue eyes. Thorn felt exposed, like she could see the fear crawling under his skin.

“Good,” she said. “Stay here until moonhigh.”

She left. Thorn was alone. The mist thickened. He imagined eyes in it, jaws, teeth. He licked his lips and held his ground.

When dawn came, he collapsed. But he had not run. That was what mattered.

**Other:** Open to plots where his fear gets someone hurt, or where he finally confronts the wolf who threw his litter into the vent.
