# NPC

**Name:** Splinter  
**Pack:** None (Rogue)  
**Rank:** Rogue (hostile loner)  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Splinter is a lean, scarred wolf with a coat of dirty gray and brown; like a splinter of rotted wood. His fur is patchy and matted, and his ribs show through. His eyes are a pale, wild yellow, darting constantly. His left foreleg is missing below the elbow; lost to a Twoleg trap years ago. He walks on three legs, but he is surprisingly fast. His ears are torn, and his muzzle is crisscrossed with old bite scars. He smells of carrion and Twoleg refuse.

**Personality:**  
*Cunning, bitter, desperate, unpredictable, secretly ashamed.*  

Splinter was not always a rogue. He was born in Greyspire, but he was cast out for killing a packmate, a fight over a piece of fresh-kill that went too far. He has been alone for two years. He has not forgiven the pack. He has not forgiven himself.

He survives by stealing from the edges of pack territories — a rabbit here, a fish there. He avoids direct confrontation. He is not brave; he is *hungry*. He attacks only when he is cornered.

**Backstory:**  
Splinter was born in Greyspire to a Lowbelly mother who died when he was a pup. He was raised in the pack, but he never belonged — he was too small, too weak, too desperate. One winter, when the pack was starving, he killed a fellow Lowbelly for a scrap of meat. He was exiled.

He has been wandering ever since. He has tried to join other packs. No one will take him. He is alone.
