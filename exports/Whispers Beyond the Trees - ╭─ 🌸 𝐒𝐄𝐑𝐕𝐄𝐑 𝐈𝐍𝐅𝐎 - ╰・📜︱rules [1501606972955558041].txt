[📝] Server Rules
**1. Follow Discord and Roblox's Terms of Service**
- All members must be at least **13 years old** (Discord's minimum age).
- Roblox ToS strictly prohibits sexual content – therefore **no sexual, adult, or NSFW roleplay is allowed anywhere**, including Discord.
- Any discussion, depiction, or roleplay of sexual acts will lead to an **immediate ban**.

**2. Age Verification**
- While this server does not host NSFW content, we may still ask for 18+ age verification to ensure a safe environment for all.
- If you are under 13, you are not allowed on Discord – any underage user discovered will be banned.

**3. Content Warnings for Sensitive Themes**
If your roleplay or discussion involves ||graphic violence, gore, torture, self-harm, suicide, addiction,|| or other potentially triggering topics, you must:
- Post a clear content warning in the channel before starting.
- Give others the chance to opt out.
- Respect that some members may be uncomfortable – do not pressure them.
Failure to provide a warning when required will result in a warning, then a temporary mute.

**4. No Jokes About Abuse or Sensitive Topics**
- Do **not** make jokes, lighthearted remarks, or casual references regarding:
  - ||Physical, emotional, or sexual abuse||
  - ||Self-harm, suicide, or trauma||
  - ||Addiction or eating disorders||
- This applies to **both IC and OOC chats** – even as "dark humor," it can be deeply hurtful to others.
- If you're unsure whether something is appropriate, err on the side of caution or ask a mod.
- Violations will result in a warning; repeat offenses will lead to a mute or kick.

**5. No Godmodding, Metagaming, or Powerplaying**
- Respect other players' agency. You control only your own character.
- Use *attempted* actions: *"X lunges for Y's throat"* – allow others to react.
- No character is invincible. Your wolf can be wounded, scared, or defeated – that's part of storytelling.
- Repeated godmodding or ignoring others' actions may lead to a kick.

**6. Separate IC (In-Character) and OOC (Out-of-Character)**
- Use `(( ))`, `//`, or `[ ]` for OOC comments within roleplay channels.
- Keep IC drama out of OOC chats (like general or voice).
- Harassment, bullying, or leaking private conversations will result in immediate moderator action (mute or ban).

**7. Character Boundaries Must Be Documented**
- Every player may list **hard limits** (e.g., no eye trauma, no humiliation, no death, etc.) in their character sheet.
- Violating another player's stated boundary is a serious offense – expect a mute or kick.
- If you are unsure about a limit, ask before proceeding.

**8. No Art Theft – Credit All Creations**
- Any artwork, character designs, writing, or other creative content you post must be either:
  - **Your own original work**, or
  - **Properly credited** to the original creator (with a link or name).
- If you commission art, credit the artist.
- Passing off others' work as your own (art theft/tracing without permission) is a **serious offense** – expect a mute or kick, and repeat offenders will be banned.

**9. AI-Generated Content – Strictly Banned**
- We value pure original creative work. **No AI-generated content is permitted anywhere** in the server.
- This includes AI-generated images, writing, character designs, lore, backstories, or any other creative material.
- If you are unsure whether a tool is AI-based, ask staff before posting.
- Violations will result in content removal and a warning; repeat offenses may lead to a mute or kick.

**10. No Real-Life Illegal Content**
- Do not discuss, depict, or share anything that violates Discord's prohibited content policy, including:
  - ||Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)||
  - ||Bestiality, necrophilia, or extreme violence||
  - ||Any illegal activity||
- Such content will be reported to Discord and, if applicable, to authorities.

**11. Character Sheets – Approval & Transparency**
- All characters must be submitted for approval before use.
- Sheets must include:
  - Name, age (must be appropriate for the setting)
  - A list of potential triggers present in the character's backstory or behaviour
  - The player's own boundaries regarding interactions
- Characters under the age of 18 are allowed for family or adventure RP but must **never** be placed in romantic or suggestive situations.

**12. Spoiler & Leak Policy – Keep Lore In-Server**
- Any lore, plot twists, character backstories, worldbuilding details, or exclusive story content shared in this server is for the community's enjoyment **here only**.
- Do **not** share, screenshot, copy-paste, or repost any server-exclusive lore or story content outside this server without express staff permission.
- Violations will be treated as a breach of trust and may result in a mute or kick.

**13. Activity, Hiatus, and Ghosting Policy**
- If you need to leave a scene unexpectedly, use `#hiatus-notice` or DM your partner to let them know.
- If you "ghost" (leave without warning) repeatedly, you may be restricted from certain channels.
- Use the **"pause"** or **"red light"** system – any participant can call a pause at any time, and the scene stops immediately without question.

**14. No Unsolicited DM Roleplay or Harassment**
- All roleplay should take place in public server channels, unless both parties explicitly agree to move to DMs.
- Unsolicited roleplay requests or harassment in DMs will result in an **instant ban**.
- Always respect when someone says "no" or "not interested".

**15. No Advertising or Self-Promotion Without Permission**
- Do not post invites to other servers, promote your own content (outside designated commission channels), or advertise services without prior staff approval.
- This also includes unsolicited DMs advertising servers, products, or external communities.
- Violators will receive a warning; repeat offenses will lead to a mute or kick.

**16. Respect Mod Decisions – Appeals Go to Private Channels**
- Our moderators enforce these rules fairly and in good faith.
- If you disagree with a moderator action, **do not argue publicly** in any channel.
- Instead, DM a staff member or use the designated `#support-tickets` channel to discuss it privately.
- Public arguments, callouts, insults, or drama regarding moderator decisions will result in a mute or escalated action.

**17. Consent for Permanent Consequences (Major Injury / Death)**
- No player may permanently kill, maim, or cause life-altering trauma to another player's character without **explicit OOC consent** beforehand.
- "In-character consequences" (like losing a fight) are fine, but permanent loss of a character must be agreed upon by all parties.
- Violating this will be treated as a serious boundary breach and may result in a kick.

**18. Keep Content in the Correct Channels – No Spam**
- Use the designated channels for their intended purpose (e.g., RP in RP channels, memes in memes, lore in lore).
- Do **not** spam pings, flood chats with repetitive messages, or post walls of text in general OOC channels.
- If you're unsure where something goes, ask a mod.
- Violations will receive a warning; repeated spam may lead to a mute.

**19. Voice Chat Conduct**
- In voice channels, be mindful of others:
  - No excessive background noise, ||ear-rape||, or loud sudden sounds.
  - Use push-to-talk if you have a noisy environment.
  - Respect that some members may not want to join VC – do not pressure them.
- Repeated VC trolling or disruptive behaviour will result in a mute or kick.

**20. Alt Accounts Must Be Registered**
- If you use an alternate Discord account to join the server, you **must** inform a staff member and link it to your main account.
- Ban evasion via alt accounts is an **instant permanent ban** for all associated accounts.
- Using alts to circumvent mutes, kicks, or role restrictions will result in escalated action.

**22. No Excessive Political or Religious Debate**
- While casual discussion is fine, do **not** engage in heated debates about politics, religion, or other divisive real-world topics in OOC channels.
- Such arguments almost always derail the community and make others uncomfortable.
- If you want to discuss these topics, take it to DMs with willing participants.
- Violations will result in a warning; repeat offenses may lead to a mute or kick.

**23. No Mini-Modding – Leave Enforcement to Staff**
- Do **not** take it upon yourself to warn, scold, or police other members for breaking rules.
- If you see a rule being broken, simply **report it to a staff member** via DM or `#reports` and let us handle it.
- Mini-modding often escalates situations unnecessarily and creates a hostile atmosphere.
- Violators will receive a warning; repeat offenses may lead to a mute.

**24. Use Common Sense – Rules Are Not Exhaustive**
- These rules cover the most common scenarios, but they cannot predict every situation.
- Staff reserve the right to take action on behaviour that violates the **spirit** of these rules, even if not explicitly listed.
- If you're unsure whether something is okay, ask a mod first.
- "I didn't see it in the rules" is not a valid defense for disruptive behaviour.

*Remember: this is a space for creativity and respect. Treat others how you wish to be treated, and help us keep it safe for everyone.*

**Consequences for Breaking Rules**
- **Warning** – for minor OOC disrespect, forgetting a content warning, or first-time AI/art credit issues.
- **Mute** – for repeated disregard of content warnings, boundary violations, minor advertising/spoiler breaches, or offensive jokes about sensitive topics.
- **Kick** – for godmodding, metagaming, harassment, serious boundary breaches, or leaking lore.
- **Ban** – for underage users, illegal content, severe harassment, non-consensual themes, or any ToS violation that endangers the community.
