# NPC

**Name:** Root  
**Pack:** Thistlehide  
**Rank:** Caretaker
**Age:** 8 years  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Root is a stout, thick-furred wolf with a coat of dark brown and deep gold — like old bark. Her fur is coarse and knotted, and she is always covered in bits of moss and twigs. Her eyes are a warm, dark amber, and she has a thick, bushy tail that she wraps around sleeping pups. Her muzzle is graying with age, and her left ear is torn (from a badger fight years ago). She moves with a slow, deliberate gait, never hurrying.

**Personality:**  
*Patient, wise, no-nonsense, deeply kind, stubborn.*  

Root has been watching Thistlehide pups for longer than any wolf remembers. She is the one who teaches them to walk, to eat solid food, to recognize the smell of a twoleg trap. She does not coddle — if a pup falls, they get up. If a pup cries, they learn to stop. But she also sits with the sick ones through the night, pressing her warm belly to their shivering bodies.

She is stubborn. She refused to let the elders kill Kanami, threatening to leave the pack if they harmed the pup. That threat carried weight — Root has raised every wolf in the pack. Without her, there would be no one to watch the pups.

**Backstory:**  
Root was born in Thistlehide to a long line of caretakers. She took over the role when she was three, after her mother died of old age. She has never left. Her mate, a hunter named Thistleclaw, died ten years ago. She still sleeps in their den, alone.

She has outlived all her pups. One died of sickness, two died in border fights, one left the pack and never returned. She does not talk about them. She shows her love by raising the pack’s pups as if they were her own grandchildren.

**Skills:**
- **Medicine (Wisdom +4):** Experienced with pup ailments — knows every herb for fever, colic, and cough.
- **Persuasion (Charisma +3):** Can calm an angry mother or a terrified pup with a few words.

**Weaknesses:**

- **Stubborn (Charisma -2):** Refuses to adapt to new ways. Clashes with younger wolves.
- **Haunted by Loss (Wisdom -3):** Her dead pups still visit her dreams.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox. She believes the Maw is the forest. She thanks it by burying a piece of every meal.

**RP Sample:**  
Root sat in the nursery den, her bushy tail wrapped around Pale’Step. The tiny white pup was shivering, her breath wheezing. Root pressed closer, letting her warmth seep into the fragile body.

“You’ll be fine,” she murmured. “You’re too stubborn to die.”

Pale’Step coughed and snuggled deeper into Root’s fur.

An hour passed. Another. Root did not move. She would stay here all night if she had to.
