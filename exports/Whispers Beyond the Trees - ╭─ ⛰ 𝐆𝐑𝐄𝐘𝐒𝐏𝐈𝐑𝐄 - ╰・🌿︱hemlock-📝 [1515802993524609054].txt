#  NPC

**Name:** Hemlock  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Medic  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Bisexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Hemlock is small for a Greyspire wolf, barely as heavy as a deer-length of caribou meat, with a reddish-brown coat that fades to cream on her belly. Her fur is always matted with dried herbs and blood: she does not care for her own appearance. One of her eyes is clouded white (cataract from a blow to the head as a pup), and she tilts her head constantly to compensate. Her paws are stained dark green from handling mountain plants. She wears a small pouch made from a squirrel hide around her neck, filled with her most precious remedies.

**Personality:**   

Hemlock is the only healer in Greyspire, and she hates it. She hates the responsibility, the screams, the wolves who die despite her best efforts. But she cannot stop. She was born with a gift for plants: a gift that marked her as *valuable* in a pack that usually kills the weak. So she stays, and she heals, and she complains about it constantly.


[6/14/2026 3:42 PM] lizardpelt
She has a sharp, mocking wit that she uses to keep others at a distance. She insults the Stoneguard to their faces, then stitches their wounds without being asked. No one knows how to take her. She likes it that way.

**Backstory:**  
Hemlock’s mother was a healer. Her grandmother was a healer. The line goes back to the Sundering, or so the stories say. But Hemlock never wanted the role. She wanted to be a hunter: to feel the wind in her fur, to chase prey across the mountains. A rockslide when she was one year old crushed her back leg, leaving her with a permanent limp. Hunting was impossible. Her mother taught her herbs instead.

She hated her mother for it. When her mother died of a respiratory infection (one Hemlock could not cure, despite her knowledge), Hemlock felt both grief and *relief*. Now she is the last of her line. She works in silence, chewing herbs, and pretending she does not dream of running.

**Skills:**
- **Herblore (Intelligence +4):** Knows every high-altitude medicinal plant. Specializes in wound-clotting and pain relief.
- **Medicine (Wisdom +4):** Excellent diagnostician; can smell infection before it shows symptoms.
- **Intimidation (Charisma +3):** Her insults are legendary. She can make a Stoneguard cry, calling him a frog-gut or carrion-breath.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Limp (Dexterity -3):** Cannot run. Cannot fight. Relies on others to bring her patients.
- **Fear of Heights (Wisdom -2):** In a mountain pack. She has panic attacks on exposed ledges.
- **Bitter (Charisma -2):** Her resentment sometimes makes her careless with patients she dislikes.

**RP Sample:**  
Hemlock chewed the moss until it was a green pulp, then spat it into the wound. The young Stoneguard yelped: dramatic, she thought, for a scratch.

“Hold still, you *vole-snout*, or I’ll sew your mouth shut instead.”

The wolf whimpered but obeyed. Hemlock worked in silence, her clouded eye drifting to the cave entrance. Snow was falling again. Another patrol would be out there, getting themselves torn up, and she would have to fix them too.

“You’re thinking too loud,” the Stoneguard muttered.

Hemlock jabbed the wound. He yelped again.

“Good. Now you’re thinking too loud to talk.” She tied off the sinew and sat back. “Rest. Don’t move. If you die, I’ll bring you back just to kill you myself, you *scat-licker*.”

She limped to the herb storage, already sorting through the dwindling supply. *Leaf-bare is coming*, she thought. *And we are not ready.*

**Other:** Open to plots involving her herb stores running out, a patient she fails to save haunting her, or a wolf trying to earn her respect.
