# NPC

**Name:** Hollowstem  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Caretaker  
**Age:** 6 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Hollowstem is a tall, thin wolf with a coat of pale tan and greenish-gray — like dead reeds. Her fur is short and water-repellent, and she is always damp. Her eyes are a warm, honey-brown, and she has a long, narrow face with a perpetually gentle expression. Her ears are large and swivel constantly, listening for pups in trouble. Her paws are long and flexible, good for digging out stuck pups. She smells of mud and milk.

**Personality:**  
*Patient, soft-spoken, fiercely protective, quietly clever, deeply maternal.*  

Hollowstem is the opposite of Mistmoor’s grim reputation. She is warm, gentle, and endlessly forgiving. Pups love her. Adults find her strange — how can she smile in a place like this? She smiles because the pups need her to. If she is sad, she hides it.

She is fiercely protective of her charges. She has stood between a rogue and the nursery den, teeth bared, despite having no fighting skill. She did not win. But she bought enough time for the guards to arrive.

**Backstory:**  
Hollowstem was born in Mistmoor to a Bog-Born mother and a Drown-Sick father. Her father was an oracle, lost in his own mind. Her mother raised her alone. Hollowstem learned early to be soft in a hard world.

She never had pups of her own — her mate died of rot-lung before they could try. She became the den-keeper instead, raising other wolves’ pups as if they were hers.

**Skills:**
- **Medicine (Wisdom +4):** Skilled at treating pup illnesses — rot-lung, fever, parasites.
- **Persuasion (Charisma +3):** Can calm a screaming pup with a whisper.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Moves quietly through the swamp, often finding lost pups before anyone else.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Non-Fighter (Strength -4):** Cannot fight. One blow would kill her.
- **Too Soft (Wisdom -2):** Hesitates to discipline pups, leading to spoiled brats.
- **Lonely (Charisma -2):** Misses having her own litter. Might make questionable decisions to keep a pup close.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox. She believes the Maw protects the innocent. She prays every sunrise.

**RP Sample:**  
Hollowstem sat in the nursery den, Mudpup curled at her side, Mosspup nestled between her paws. The den was damp and dark, but the pups were warm. She hummed a low, tuneless lullaby — one her mother used to sing.

Mudpup’s ears twitched. “The Maw is chewing tonight.”

Hollowstem paused. “Is it?”

“Yes. Loud.”

She did not know what to say to that. So she kept humming, hoping the sound would drown out the chewing.
