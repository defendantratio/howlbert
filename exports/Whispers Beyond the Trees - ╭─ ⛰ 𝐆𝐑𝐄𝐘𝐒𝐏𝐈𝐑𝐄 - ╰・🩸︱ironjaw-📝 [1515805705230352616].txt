# NPC

**Name:** Ironjaw  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Hunter  
**Age:** 5 years  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Ironjaw is a compact, powerful wolf with a smoky-gray coat that lightens to silver on his underbelly. His shoulders are heavily muscled, his neck thick, his jaws strong. His most notable feature is his *ashes* he rolls in volcanic ash from a nearby vent, giving his fur a permanent gray-white dusting that masks his scent on the wind. His eyes are a warm brown, unusual for Greyspire, and he has a calm, steady gaze. His left hind leg has a slight twist (old break, poorly healed).

**Personality:**

Ironjaw is the best hunter in Greyspire. He does not brag about this. He does not talk much at all. He prefers the company of the mountains to the company of wolves. When he is not hunting, he sits alone on high ledges, watching the clouds.

He is kind — as kind as a Greyspire wolf can be. He shares his kills with the Lowbellies without being asked. He has never challenged anyone for rank. He seems content to simply *be*. But there is a sadness in his brown eyes, a loneliness he cannot name.

**Backstory:**  
Ironjaw was born to a Stoneguard father and a hunter mother. His father wanted him to be a guard. His mother wanted him to hunt. He chose hunting: not out of preference, but because it meant spending less time around other wolves. He is not antisocial. He simply… does not connect. He has tried. He has failed.

When he was three, he fell during a hunt: a goat kicked him, and he tumbled down a ravine. His leg broke. He lay in the snow for three days before a patrol found him. The leg healed wrong. He hunts differently now, relying on ambush rather than pursuit. He never hunts goats again.

**Skills:**
- **Hunting (Strength +4):** Expert at close-range ambush and neck-snap kills.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** The volcanic ash mask makes him nearly scentless.
- **Survival (Wisdom +3):** Can endure days without food, sleeping in snow dens.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Old Leg Injury (Dexterity -3):** Cannot chase fleeing prey. Must kill quickly or lose the hunt.
- **Socially Withdrawn (Charisma -3):** Other wolves find him unsettling. He has no allies.
- **Secret Guilt (Wisdom -2):** He blames himself for the patrol that found him; one of them died in an avalanche during the rescue.

**Maw-Belief:** Agnostic — he has seen too much suffering to believe in a plan. He hunts because the pack must eat. That is enough.

**RP Sample:**  
The goat did not see him. Ironjaw had buried himself in ash and snow, his smoky coat invisible against the gray-white slope. He waited. The goat took another step. Another.

Now.

He exploded from the snow, jaws closing on the goat’s throat. The animal kicked, once, twice, but Ironjaw’s weight and the angle of his bite crushed the windpipe. The goat went down.

He lay there for a moment, breathing in the scent of blood and ash. Then he began the long drag back to the den. His hind leg ached. It always ached.

A young wolf ran up to help him. Ironjaw shook his head. He did not want help. He wanted to be alone with the fresh-kill and the mountain and the quiet.

The young wolf stepped back, confused. Ironjaw did not explain. He never explained.

**Other:** Open to plots where someone tries to break through his isolation, or where the avalanche victim’s family seeks revenge.
