# NPC

**Name:** Fernspot  
**Pack:** Thistlehide  
**Rank:** Forager  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Fernspot is a small, roundish wolf with a coat of dusty brown and pale green, like dried ferns. Her fur is short and soft, and she has a scattering of darker spots across her shoulders. Her eyes are a warm, frightened amber, and she has a habit of looking over her shoulder constantly. Her paws are small and neat. She moves with a nervous, twitchy energy, never still.

**Personality:**  
*Anxious, remorseful, secretive, soft-spoken, desperate for approval.*  

Fernspot was the one who agreed to the abandonment. She tells herself she had no choice; the elders said the pup was cursed, that the pack would starve. But she knows she had a choice. She made the wrong one.

She cries in secret, when no one is watching. She has tried to approach Kanami twice, to say something, anything, but each time she lost her nerve. She leaves small piles of edible roots near Kanami’s hiding spots, pretending they are accidental.


[6/15/2026 6:53 AM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Fernspot was born in Thistlehide to a forager mother and a hunter father. She was never ambitious; she wanted a mate, a litter, a quiet life. When Kanami was born blind and marked, Fernspot felt a flicker of love — and then terror. The elders’ words burned in her ears: *curse, rot, death*. She let Ashbark take the pup.

She has not forgiven herself. She does not expect Kanami to forgive her either.

**Skills:**
- **Foraging (Intelligence +3):** Knows every edible root and berry in Thistlehide.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Can move quietly, a skill she uses to leave offerings for Kanami unseen.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Guilt (Wisdom -3):** Paralyzed by remorse. Cannot confront what she did.
- **Anxious (Wisdom -2):** Always looking over her shoulder. Afraid of being exposed.
- **Secretive (Charisma -2):** Her hidden offerings could be misunderstood as something else.

**Statistics (Total: 17 — Forager range 15–20)**  
Strength: 3 (–2)  
Dexterity: 5 (0)  
Survival: 5 (0)  
Intelligence: 5 (0)  
Charisma: 3 (–2)  
Wisdom: 4 (–1)

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox (but she prays for forgiveness constantly, never feeling like she is heard).

**RP Sample:**  
Fernspot crept through the undergrowth, a bundle of feverfew roots in her jaws. She stopped at the edge of a small clearing. Kanami was there, sitting motionless beneath a hazel bush. Fernspot dropped the roots and backed away.

A twig snapped. Kanami’s head turned.

“Who’s there?”

Fernspot froze. She did not answer. She could not. She slipped away into the ferns, heart pounding, leaving the roots behind.

**Other:**  
- **Mate:** Ashbark. They sleep in the same den but do not speak of Kanami.  
- **Open plots:** Kanami could discover her offerings; Fernspot might confess; she could be ostracized if the truth comes out.
