# NPC

**Name:** Moth  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Lowbelly  
**Age:** 2 years  
**Sexuality:** Bisexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Moth is a small, pale-furred wolf with a coat of dusty gray and white;  the color of moth wings. Her fur is thin and soft, and she has a habit of pressing herself flat against the ground when frightened. Her eyes are a large, dark brown, always wide with fear. Her ears are intact (rare for a Lowbelly), and she has a small notch in her tail (lost to frostbite). She trembles constantly.

**Personality:**  
*Timid, desperate to please, clever, haunted, secretly hopeful.*  

Moth is a survivor. She was born a Lowbelly, but she does not want to die one. She watches the Stoneguard, studies their techniques, learns their weaknesses. She bides her time. One day, she will challenge someone — and win.

But she is not cruel. She shares her food with pups even when she is starving. She grooms injured wolves even when no one is watching. She wants to be good. She just does not know how to escape her circumstances.

**Backstory:**  
Moth was born to a Lowbelly mother who was killed by a Stoneguard for stealing food. Moth watched it happen. She has been trying to be invisible ever since.

She has never challenged anyone. She is not strong enough. But she practices fighting in secret, in the dark, when no one can see. She dreams of a day when the Lowbellies rise up.
