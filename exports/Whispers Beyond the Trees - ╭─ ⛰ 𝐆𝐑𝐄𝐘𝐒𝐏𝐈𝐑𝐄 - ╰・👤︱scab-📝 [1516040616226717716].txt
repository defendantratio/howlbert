# NPC

**Name:** Scab  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Lowbelly  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Scab is a thin, mange-ridden wolf with a coat of patchy brown and gray; his fur falls out in clumps, revealing scabbed skin underneath. His eyes are a dull, yellow-brown, and he has a permanent sneer on his scarred muzzle. His tail is broken and crooked (old injury). His ears are torn to shreds. He smells of infection and old blood.

**Personality:**  
*Bitter, desperate, cunning, unreliable, secretly terrified.*  

Scab has been a Lowbelly his entire life. He was born a Lowbelly, and he will die a Lowbelly. He hates the pack, hates the Stoneguard, hates the Highfang. But he is not brave enough to leave. So he stays, and he scrapes by, and he nurses his hatred in the dark.

He is not completely useless. He knows things: secrets, hidden passages, which guards can be bribed with extra food. He trades information for survival. No one trusts him. He trusts no one.

**Backstory:**  
Scab was born to a Lowbelly mother who died of infection when he was a pup. He was raised by the pack’s caretakers, who fed him last and hit him often. He learned to fight dirty, to steal, to lie.

He has tried to challenge for a higher rank twice. He lost both times. The second time, the Stoneguard captain broke his tail as a lesson. He has not tried again.
