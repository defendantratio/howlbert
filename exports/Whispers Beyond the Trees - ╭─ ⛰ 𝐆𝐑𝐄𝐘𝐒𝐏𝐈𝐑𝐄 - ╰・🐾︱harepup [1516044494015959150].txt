Harepup is a slender, long-legged pup with a coat of pale silver-gray and white — like winter moss on granite. Her fur is short and sleek, and her ears are noticeably large, swiveling constantly. Her eyes are a bright, curious amber, and she has a thin white scar across her nose from a fight with a fox cub. Her tail is long and expressive, always in motion. She is already fast — faster than any pup her age — and she knows it.

**Personality:**  
*Competitive, sharp-tongued, loyal, impatient, secretly lonely.*  

Harepup is the fastest pup in Greyspire, and she never lets anyone forget it. She races everyone — adults, pups, even the elders — and crows when she wins. She is loud, brash, and obnoxious. Other pups avoid her. She pretends not to care.

She cares deeply. She wants friends, but she does not know how to be soft. Her sharp tongue pushes wolves away before they can leave her first. Her mother died when she was two moons old; her father is distant and cold. She has learned that the only way to be noticed is to be the best.

**Backstory:**  
Harepup’s mother died of a respiratory infection when Harepup was two moons old. Her father, a Stoneguard, threw himself into his duties and left Harepup to be raised by the pack’s den-keepers. She has been hungry for attention ever since.

She earned her name because she runs like a hare — fast, erratic, impossible to catch. The elders say the Maw gave her speed to make up for her small size. Harepup is not sure she believes in the Maw, but she likes the idea that she was *gifted*.
