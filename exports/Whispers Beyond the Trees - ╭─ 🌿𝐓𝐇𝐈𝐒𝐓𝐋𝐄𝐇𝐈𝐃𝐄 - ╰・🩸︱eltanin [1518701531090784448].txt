**Backstory:** 
In a small den, belonging to the Thistlehide pack three pups were born during a the rampant roars of a storm. Eltanin was the only pup in her litter to survive after birth, her siblings were culled due to severe deformity. Eltanin was born large and loud, her mother having torn something during the birth, ended up passing due to the trauma.

Eltanin was always quiet, she spent most of her puphood silently observing others. She often refused to play and instead would stalk small critters, and mind her own business. As an adult, she's still pretty distant but is more open to socializing.

Eltanin, although raised around others who believed in the Maw has decided that belief doesn't quite 'fit' with her.. She is never vocal about her beliefs and doesn't outright reject the Maw, more so leaning towards 'there's no proof' of it. Eltanin is open to her beliefs changing as she goes on through life.

Eltanin has had very few human interactions. She outright avoids them, thinking them ugly and uncanny looking. She's never been close to one, and refuses to be anywhere nearby when the distant bang from their thunder-sticks go off.
