# NPC

**Name:** Mossgaze  
**Pack:** Thistlehide  
**Rank:** Forager  
**Age:** 2 years (young wolf)  
**Sexuality:** Asexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Mossgaze is small by Thistlehide standards, weighing roughly as much as a half-grown deer, with a dusty russet-brown coat that darkens to tawny along her flanks and belly. Her fur grows thick and shaggy in winter but lies short and sleek in summer, following the pack's seasonal pattern. Her eyes are pale, washed-out amber set in a darker face, giving her a perpetually startled look. A thin white scar cuts through her left eyebrow and across the bridge of her nose from a childhood thorn thicket, and her left ear is notched twice in the way that comes from pushing through brambles. Her paws are unusually large and splayed, clumsy on flat ground but nimble on the tangled root-heaves of Thistlehide territory.

**Personality:**  
Watchful, quiet, brittle, loyal, self-effacing.  

Mossgaze speaks in short bursts and often trails off mid-sentence, as if she has forgotten she was allowed to speak at all. She tracks every exit from every clearing and reads the mood of a group before she enters it, calibrated to avoid attention. Among the foragers of Thistlehide she is competent but unremarkable, her plant knowledge precise but her confidence easily shattered by a harsh word.

Around her brother Finn’pelt, she becomes almost invisible: head low, tail neutral, no eye contact. Strangers assume she fears him. In truth, she understands him perfectly and plays her role without complaint because she knows what happens to wolves who are seen as soft spots. Alone with him in the deep mist before dawn, she will sit close enough that their shoulders nearly touch. Neither speaks. That is their conversation.

**Backstory:**  
Mossgaze was born in Thistlehide to a low-ranking hunter mother who died of infection when Mossgaze was six moons old. Her brother Finn’pelt was already grown and already carving his reputation with scarred knuckles and a face like chipped stone. Most wolves assumed he would let the runt of the litter die. Instead, he began leaving half-killed rabbits at the den entrance — never seen, never acknowledged, and never spoken of since.

When Mossgaze was ten moons old, a group of older pups cornered her in a ravine and held her down, demanding she eat foxglove to prove she was "brave enough to be a real wolf." She refused. They forced her anyway. She survived by making herself vomit immediately, but the experience cracked something in her: not fear of death, but fear of other wolves. She has never told Finn’pelt. She knows what he would do to those pups, and she has chosen to carry the silence instead.

She believes in the Maw the same way she believes in leaf-bare: it exists, it is hungry, and the only sensible response is to stay small and stay out of its way. She has never felt its presence directly and suspects it has better things to do than notice her. As for twolegs, she encountered a deadfall trap as a yearling, a rusted iron jaw from a decade-dead hunter that caught her by the leg. She chewed her own fur off to escape and still carries a ring of scar tissue around her right ankle. She has never seen a living human, and she prays to the Maw she never will.

**Skills:**
- **Foraging (Intelligence +4):** Expert knowledge of edible and medicinal plants in misty, boggy, thorny terrain.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Moves quietly and quickly over wet stone, mud, and root-tangled ground; comfortable pushing through thistle and bramble where larger wolves hesitate.
- **Escape (Dexterity +3):** Excellent at squeezing through tight spaces and vanishing into undergrowth.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Small and Frail (Strength -4):** Cannot fight larger wolves or predators; will lose any direct confrontation.
- **Social Anxiety (Charisma -3):** Freezes or stumbles when addressed by authority figures, making her seem incompetent.
- **Overly Self-Reliant (Wisdom -2):** Will hide injuries rather than ask for help, which has nearly killed her twice.

**Statistics**  
Strength: 4 (–1)  
Dexterity: 7 (+1)  
Survival (Constitution): 5 (0)  
Intelligence: 8 (+2)  
Charisma: 4 (–1)  
Wisdom: 6 (+1)

**Maw-Belief:** Agnostic — she acknowledges the Maw’s existence the same way she acknowledges leaf-bare, but she does not pray or make offerings.

**Other:**  
- **Family ties:** Younger sibling of Finn’pelt. He treats her with public coldness but is secretly hyper-protective. She is one of the few wolves who has seen him vulnerable.  
- **Open plots:** Her survival creates constant tension for Finn’pelt: any injury she suffers, any witness to their private dynamic, any debt she owes to another Thistlehide wolf could force him to choose between his reputation and his loyalty to her. If Finn’pelt takes a mate, Mossgaze will watch that wolf with quiet, terrible attention.
