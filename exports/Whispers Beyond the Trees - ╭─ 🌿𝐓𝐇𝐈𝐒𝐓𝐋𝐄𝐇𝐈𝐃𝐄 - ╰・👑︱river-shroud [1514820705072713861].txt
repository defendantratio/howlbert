**Appearance:**

River’Shroud is a shockingly massive adult she-wolf, towering over most packmates due to her rare, heavy-boned build and brick-like silhouette. Her dense, coarse fur is a striking patchwork of black, white, and deep slate gray, growing exceptionally thick in winter to deflect the sharpest forest thorns. Her most defining feature is a set of darkened, soot-stained deer antlers lodged firmly atop her head, angled back like a crown: a prized trophy she refuses to take off from a brutal, successful hunt. The antlers’ base is wedged into the thick fur between her ears, held in place by matted hair and dried sap. Her broad muzzle and heavy chest are crisscrossed with deep scars, while her unblinking eyes are a cool, piercing pale gray like winter river ice.


**Personality:**

Impossibly calm, stoic, severely shy and socially anxious, an unfazed anchor, fiercely protective, a silent guardian.

River’Shroud presents herself as a wall of absolute ice. Because her vocal cords were ruined in her youth, she communicates almost entirely through heavy, unblinking stares, deep chest rumbles, or dismissive exhales. She is intensely uncomfortable around crowds and lacks the social grace for the pack's casual gossip, political posturing, or traditional storytelling, using her freezing, silent aura to keep others at a distance.

Beneath her intimidating exterior lies a deep well of loyalty and a desperate need to protect. She cannot express affection with words, so she shows it through action — standing between danger and those she loves, enduring any hardship without complaint, and silently providing for her foster pup, Pale’Step.

**Backstory:**

River’Shroud was born directly into Thistlehide. As a small pup, she contracted a brutal throat infection that permanently scarred her vocal cords, leaving her voice a faint, raspy gravel that she despises using. Because of her silence and intense shyness, she spent her youth isolated from her peers, pouring every ounce of her frustration and energy into intense physical conditioning, mapping territory changes, and mastering silent border movements.

Her legendary physical strength is a direct result of her defining emotional Wound. A few seasons ago, her mother was running the borders when a team of twoleg logging trucks raced down the thunderpath at night, their blinding eyes and deafening roar disorienting the patrol. Her mother was struck and pinned beneath an unstable logging pile near the highway. Despite a desperate, agonizing struggle, River’Shroud was not strong enough to lift the crushing wood, and her mother died before her eyes. The failure shattered her, leaving her with a hollow grief and an obsessive, desperate drive to build her physical power so she never fails to protect anyone again.


[6/14/2026 5:44 PM] lizardpelt
This loss has been compounded by the terrifying escalation of the pipeline survey in Thistlehide territory. River’Shroud has become a frequent participant in the midnight raids on the survey camps, using her immense size to tear out white survey stakes and rip apart twoleg materials. To prove her strength to the pack after her mother's death, she took down a massive, aggressive stag near the logging lines completely solo, snapping its neck and claiming its darkened, ash-stained antlers as a permanent prize. She wedged them onto her head that same night and has never taken them off. The recent death of a pack pup from the humans' retaliatory poison baits has pushed her protective instincts to the absolute brink, making her fiercely determined to keep her foster pup, Pale’Step, locked deep within the hidden dens away from the impending slaughter. She believes deeply in the Thistlehide tradition that the forest is the Maw’s coat and wolves are the biting fleas, but her ruined voice prevents her from joining the pack's roars. Instead, she honors the Maw by burying a piece of meat from every single kill beneath the oldest tree roots in total silence.

**Maw-Belief:**

Orthodox — but silent. She believes the forest is the Maw’s fur and that wolves are the fleas biting deep. She cannot howl her prayers, so she honors the Maw through ritual: burying a piece of every fresh-kill beneath the oldest tree roots, standing motionless in the rain to feel the Maw’s tears, and pressing her antlers to the earth after a hunt. Her faith is private, physical, and unshakable.


**RP Sample**

The mist hung low over the Thistlehide border, cold and wet as river fog. River’Shroud stood motionless among the ferns, her antlered silhouette merging with the twisted branches of an old oak. Her pale gray eyes tracked the treeline without blinking.

A twig snapped.

She did not flinch. Her chest rumbled — a low, warning growl that vibrated through the ground.

A young scout — Mossheart— emerged from the undergrowth, saw her, and froze. His ears flattened. His tail tucked.

She tilted her head. *What?*

“G-Greyspire patrol,” he stammered. “Three wolves. Heading south. Finn’pelt wants you at the ridge.”

River’Shroud stared at him for a long moment. Then she turned and padded toward the ridge, her massive paws silent on the wet earth. She did not look back to see if he followed. She did not need to.

When she reached the ridge, she crouched low, her belly pressing into the frost. Below her, three gray shapes moved through the valley. She counted them. Memorized their scents. Then she settled in to wait, her antlers gleaming dully in the moonlight, a silent sentinel carved from stone and grief.
