# NPC

**Name:** Raven
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Scout  
**Age:** 2 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Raven is a slender, dark-furred wolf with a coat so black it seems to absorb light. His paws are noticeably larger than the rest of his body: a juvenile feature he has not grown into yet. His eyes are a bright, curious amber, and his ears are constantly swiveling, tracking sounds. A small notch in his left ear is his only scar (from a territorial dispute with a fox). He moves with an eager, jittery energy, never quite still.

**Personality:**    

Raven is young and knows it. He talks too much, asks too many questions, and smiles at wolves who would rather eat him. But he is also *good* at his job; his large paws distribute his weight on loose stone, and his dark coat makes him nearly invisible at night. He wants to be Stoneguard one day, but he knows he is not strong enough. So he runs the ridges and brings back news, hoping Grim will notice him.

He has a secret: he is terrified of the dark. Not of the mountains at night, he has memorized every ledge, but of the *something* he sometimes sees in the shadows. Shapes without form. Eyes without faces. He tells no one.

**Backstory:**  
Raven was born to a hunter and a forager, neither of whom expected him to survive. He was the runt, small and sickly. But he grew, slowly, unevenly, into a wolf with legs too long and paws too big. He could not fight, but he could *run*. The previous scout took him under his wing, taught him the ridges, the hiding spots, the ways of the wind.

The scout died last leaf-bare; fell from a cliff during a storm. Raven found the body. He did not howl. He just memorized the spot and kept running. That is what his mentor would have wanted.

**RP Sample:**  
Raven flattened himself against the rock, his dark fur blending with the shadow of the outcropping. Below him, a Silverrush patrol moved along the river: three wolves, talking in low voices. He counted them. Memorized their scents.

One of them looked up. Raven stopped breathing.

The moment stretched. Then the Silverrush wolf looked away, and Raven exhaled. He waited until the patrol was gone, then slipped back down the mountain, his large paws finding holds he could not see.

When he reached the Greyspire den, Grim was waiting.

“Well?”

“Silverrush is nervous,” Raven said. “They’re sending extra patrols to the mill ruins. They’re scared of something.”

Grim’s eyes narrowed. “Good.”

Raven did not ask why. He just nodded and went to find something to eat. He was hungry. He was always hungry.

**Other:** Open to plots where he discovers something he should not, or where his fear of the dark leads him into danger.

**Skills:**
- **Stealth (Dexterity +4):** His dark coat and natural grace make him nearly invisible in shadow. Moves across scree and loose stone without a sound.

- **Talks Too Much (Charisma -3):** Cannot stop asking questions. Drives older wolves mad. Sometimes talks himself into trouble.
- **Fear of the Dark (Wisdom -3):** Terrified of the shadows. The shapes he sees at night, faceless, formless, make him freeze. He hides it well, but when pressed, he breaks.
