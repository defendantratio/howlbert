# NPC

**Name:** Gasp  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Drown-Sick Oracle (formerly Bog-Born)  
**Age:** 4 years  
**Sexuality:** Asexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Non-binary (they/them)

**Appearance:**  
Gasp is a gaunt, skeletal wolf with a coat of pale gray and greenish-white — like bleached bones and swamp mist. Their fur is patchy and thin, revealing translucent skin and visible ribs. Their eyes are a milky, clouded white — completely blind, but they seem to *look* at things anyway. Their mouth hangs slightly open, and they make a soft, wet gasping sound with every breath, hence their name. Their paws are long and bony, with too many toes (a birth defect). They smell of stagnant water and decay.

**Personality:**  
*Eerie, detached, prophetic, gentle (in a strange way), deeply lonely.*  

Gasp fell into the Belly-Rip two years ago. They were down there for three days; longer than any other Drown-Sick. When they were pulled out, they were *changed*. They speak in riddles, often answering questions that have not been asked yet. They have no memory of their life before the fall.

Other wolves avoid them. They are unsettling — they stare with those blind eyes, they whisper prophecies that always come true, they laugh at things no one else can hear. But they are not cruel. They have never hurt anyone. They just want to be left alone.

**Backstory:**  
Before the fall, Gasp was a quiet, unremarkable Bog-Born wolf named Reed. They had no special skills, no ambitions, no mates. They simply *existed*. Then they slipped on a wet log and tumbled into the Belly-Rip.

For three days, they floated in the dark, listening to the chewing. When they emerged, they were not Reed anymore. They were Gasp. They have been an oracle ever since.

**Skills:**
- **Prophecy (Wisdom +4):** Their cryptic predictions often come true. No one knows how.
- **Perception (Wisdom +3):** Can hear things from impossible distances — including the Belly-Rip's whispers.
- **Stealth (Dexterity +3):** Moves like a ghost. Often appears without warning.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Frail (Strength -4):** Cannot fight. Cannot hunt. Cannot run. Relies on the pack to feed them.
- **Unsettling (Charisma -3):** Wolves avoid them. They have no allies.
- **Dissociative (Wisdom -3):** Often lost in visions. Forgets to eat, sleep, or speak for days.

**RP Sample:**  
Gasp sat at the edge of the Belly-Rip, their blind eyes turned toward the dark water. The chewing was loud today — a wet, rhythmic grinding that made their bones ache. They pressed their nose to the mud and listened.

A voice behind them. “Gasp. The Rotmother wants a prophecy.”

They did not turn. “Tell her the moon will blink twice before the snow melts.”

“What does that mean?”

“It means she will understand when it happens.”

The messenger left. Gasp stayed, listening, waiting for the next whisper.
