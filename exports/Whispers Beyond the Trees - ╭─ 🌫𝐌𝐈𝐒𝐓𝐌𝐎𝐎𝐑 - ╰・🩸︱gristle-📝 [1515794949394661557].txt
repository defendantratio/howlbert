# NPC

**Name:** Gristle  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Hunter  
**Age:** 6 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Gristle is a thick-necked, broad-chested wolf with a coat of coarse, wiry fur the color of dead bracken: brown, gray, and faded orange. His fur is always crusted with dried blood and mud, which he makes no effort to clean. His eyes are a flat, dark brown, set deep in a scarred face. His left ear is a ragged stump (torn off by a boar), and his muzzle is crossed with old bite scars. He is missing three claws on his right forepaw (lost to frostbite during a leaf-bare hunt). He smells of old carrion and swamp gas.

**Personality:**  

Gristle is not subtle. He does not ambush. He *charges*. He crashes through reeds, splashes through shallows, and drags down prey with raw strength and stubbornness. Other hunters despise him for scaring away half the prey in the territory. But when the pack is starving, Gristle is the one who comes back with meat.

He is foul-mouthed and ill-tempered. He calls pups frog-guts and vole-snouts as terms of endearment. He picks fights with larger wolves and loses, then gets up and does it again. He is not smart. He is not graceful. He is *useful*.


[6/14/2026 3:11 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Gristle was born a Lowbelly in Greyspire, before he fled to Mistmoor. He does not talk about why he left. Some say he killed a Stoneguard in a fair fight and was exiled. Others say he was caught stealing from the fresh-kill cache. He never corrects them.

He came to Mistmoor six years ago, half-dead, and was taken in by the Rotmother at the time. He has repaid that debt a hundred times over with meat. He still feels like an outsider. He still expects to be thrown out. So he hunts harder than anyone, hoping that usefulness will earn him a place.


**Skills:**
- **Hunting (Strength +3):** Brutal, direct, bull-headed. Takes down prey twice his size by refusing to let go.
- **Intimidation (Charisma +2):** His scars and stench make smaller prey freeze in fear.
- **Endurance (Constitution +4):** Can run for a quarter-moon on half a vole. Refuses to quit.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Loud (Dexterity -2):** Scares prey away. Other hunters hate hunting with him.
- **Missing Claws (Dexterity -2):** Reduced grip on slippery banks. Sometimes loses his footing.
- **Ashamed of His Past (Charisma -3):** Will fly into a rage if anyone mentions Greyspire.

**RP Sample:**  
Gristle charged. The boar heard him coming — of course it did, he sounded like a rockslide — and turned to meet him. Tusks lowered. Gristle did not slow.

He took a tusk in the shoulder. It went deep. He did not stop. He clamped his jaws on the boar’s throat and *pulled*. The boar squealed, thrashed, dragged him through the mud. Gristle held on.

When the boar finally collapsed, Gristle let go. He stood over the carcass, bleeding from his shoulder, and snarled at the other hunters who had been watching from a safe distance.

*“Fresh-kill,”* he spat. *“Now help me drag it, you scat-lickers.”*

He limped back to the den, leaving a trail of blood and bristles. He did not ask for the healer. He never asked.

**Other:** Open to plots where his Greyspire past catches up to him, or where his refusal to ask for help finally kills him.
