**Appearance:**  
Rottedust is a small, hunched wolf with a coat of greenish-brown and sickly yellow — like rotting fungus. Eir fur is patchy and moist, and ey always smells of decay. Eir eyes are a pale, cloudy amber, and ey has a permanent tremor in eir left paw (nerve damage from a spider bite). Eir ears are ragged and bitten. Ey moves with a slow, shuffling gait, often stopping to examine rotting logs or mushroom patches.

**Personality:**  
*Obsessive, brilliant, socially awkward, gentle, morbid.*  

Rottedust is fascinated by death. Not killing — ey has never hurt another wolf — but the *process* of decay: how a body returns to the swamp, how fungus blooms from rot, how the Maw digests what it is given. Ey collects dead things: beetles, frogs, mice. Ey dries them and stores them in a hollow log.

Other wolves find ey unsettling. Ey does not mind. Ey has eir dead things and eir herbs. That is enough.

**Backstory:**  
Rottedust was born with a tremor. Eir mother, a Bog-Born, tried to drown ey in the swamp when ey was a pup — ey was too strange, too creepy. Ey survived by biting eir mother’s nose and escaping into the reeds.

Mirewort found ey a day later, half-drowned, clutching a dead frog. He did not ask questions. He took ey back to the den and began teaching ey to identify rot-lung herbs. Rottedust has been eir apprentice ever since.
