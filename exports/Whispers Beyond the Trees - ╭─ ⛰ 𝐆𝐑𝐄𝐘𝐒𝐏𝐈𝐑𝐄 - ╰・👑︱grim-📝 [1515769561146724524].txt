# NPC
**Name:** Grim
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Alpha (Highfang)  
**Age:** 7 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Grim is a mountain of a wolf, even by Greyspire standards: broad-chested, thick-necked, with a gray-blue coat that fades to near-black on his paws and muzzle. His left ear is torn to a ragged stump, and a mesh of old scars crisscrosses his shoulders like a second pelt. His eyes are a pale, icy yellow, and he has a habit of narrowing them to slits when judging another wolf’s worth. A chunk of his tail is missing: lost to a rockslide he survived as a young wolf. He moves with a deliberate, heavy gait, as if the mountain itself walks.

**Personality:**  

Grim did not become Highfang by being the strongest, though he is strong. He became Highfang by being the *cruelest at the right moments* and the *most generous at the right moments*. He believes the pack is a blade: it must be sharpened on the bones of the weak. He despises mercy but respects *usefulness*. A wolf who can hunt but cannot fight is still useful. A wolf who can fight but cannot keep his mouth shut is dead.  

He is paranoid about the other packs, especially after the mill expedition. He thinks the Pact of the Remembered is a trick by Thistlehide to soften Greyspire’s teeth. Yet he secretly envies Finn’pelt’s ability to inspire loyalty without fear. Grim rules through terror and bread: a careful balance he maintains by never showing the same face twice.


[6/14/2026 1:36 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Grim was born a Lowbelly: the lowest of the low. His mother was a Skeleton-Tooth elder culled during a hard leaf-bare. His father was unknown. He survived by being *useful* to the Stoneguard, carrying water, cleaning dens, eating last. When he was three, he challenged a Stoneguard captain for his rank — not fairly, but with a rock hidden in his paw. The captain died. Grimclaw took his place.  

The previous Highfang, Old Blight (no relation), saw something in him: not honor, but *hunger*. Grim killed Blight in his sleep on the night of the blood-red moon, claiming it was a fair challenge. No one dared dispute him. He has ruled for four years, and the mountains have never been quieter, or more tense.

**Skills:**
- **Intimidation (Charisma +4):** Can silence a howling pack with a look.
- **Hunting (Strength +3):** Expert at ambushing mountain goats on narrow ledges.
- **Survival (Wisdom +3):** Knows every cave and water source in the Spinefang range.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Paranoia (Wisdom -3):** Sees betrayal in every shadow. Has executed two wolves on suspicion alone.
- **Old Injury (Dexterity -2):** Missing tail chunk affects balance on steep climbs.
- **No Heirs (Wisdom -2):** Has no mate, no pups, no clear successor. The pack will tear itself apart when he dies.

**Maw-Belief:** Orthodox — but he believes the Maw’s hunger is a *test*. Wolves who die feed the mountain. Wolves who survive prove they are worth keeping.

**RP Sample:**  
The cave was cold, but Grim did not shiver. He sat on the stone throne — a natural rock shelf worn smooth by generations of Highfangs, and watched the scout tremble before him.

“Repeat it,” he said. His voice was gravel and frost.

“Th-the Thistlehide alpha… Finn’pelt… he’s calling a gathering at the Sundering Stone. All packs invited. To… to remember the dead.”

Grim’s pale yellow eyes narrowed. “Remember the dead.” He tasted the words. “The dead are carrion. The living are what matters.”

He stood, his massive frame blocking the firelight. “Send word to Silverrush. Tell their Current I want a private meeting. Before the gathering. And tell no one else.”

The scout scrambled away. Grim stared at the cave wall, where a single claw mark represented every wolf he had killed. There were many.

**Other:** Open to plots involving betrayal, succession crises, or secret alliances with Silverrush.
