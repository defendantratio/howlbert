# NPC

**Name:** Ripplepup  
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Pup  
**Age:** 4 moons  
**Sexuality:** Too young  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Ripplepup is a small, sleek pup with a coat of pale silver and white — like moonlight on shallow water. Her fur is short and water-repellent, and she is always damp from splashing in the shallows. Her eyes are a deep, navy blue — almost purple in certain light — and she has a small, dark patch on her hip shaped like a fish skeleton. Her paws are slightly webbed, a sign of strong Silverrush bloodline. She moves with a wobbly, excited energy.

**Personality:**  
*Energetic, fearless, nosy, loyal, easily distracted.*  

Ripplepup is the pup who asks strangers their names, who tries to befriend crows, who chases minnows in the shallows until she is shivering with cold. She is not afraid of anything — not the deep water, not the older wolves, not the stories about the Maw. Her mother says she has no sense of self-preservation. Ripplepup thinks that is a compliment.

She is intensely curious about the mill ruins and the strange red color the river turned during the awakening. She collects rusted iron scraps and hoards them in a hidden cache. She does not know why. She just feels *drawn* to them.

**Backstory:**  
Ripplepup was born in Silverrush to a hunter mother and a diplomat father. She is the only surviving pup of her litter — her siblings were swept away by a flash flood when she was two moons old. She survived by clinging to a fallen log. She does not remember it. Her mother does.

She has a strange connection to the river. She can feel the current shift before it changes. She knows when the fish are coming. The elders say she is touched by the Maw. Ripplepup does not know what that means, but she likes the sound of it.

**RP Sample:**  
Ripplepup splashed through the shallows, her silver fur dark with water. Ahead of her, a piece of rusted iron glinted in the mud. She dove for it, grabbed it in her teeth, and surfaced with a triumphant squeak.

“Another one?” A voice from the bank. Driftkit, her only friend.

“Another one!” Ripplepup paddled to shore and dropped the iron at Driftpup’s paws. “I’m going to build a mountain.”

“It’s just a pile of rust.”

“It’s a *mountain*,” Ripplepup insisted. “And when it’s tall enough, I’m going to climb it and howl at the moon.”

Driftpup sighed. But she helped carry the iron back to the hiding spot.
