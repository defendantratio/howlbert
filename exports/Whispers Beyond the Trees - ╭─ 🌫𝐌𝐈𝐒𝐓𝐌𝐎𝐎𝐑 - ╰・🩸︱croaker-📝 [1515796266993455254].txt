# NPC

**Name:** Croaker  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Hunter (specializes in frogs, fish, and small prey)  
**Age:** 2 years (young adult)  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Croaker is a small, wiry wolf, barely larger than a coyote, with a coat of pale, speckled brown and greenish-gray, like the back of a leopard frog. His fur is smooth and damp, and his eyes are a bright, bulging yellow-green that seem to look in different directions at once. His toes are long and flexible, almost like fingers, and he can splay them to grip wet logs. He has a small, upturned nose and a nervous, twitching tail. He makes a soft *croaking* sound in his throat when he is concentrating: hence the name.

**Personality:**   

Croaker is a specialist. He does not hunt deer or boar. He hunts frogs, fish, water-rat, and the occasional snake. He is the best in Mistmoor at catching the small, slippery prey that keeps the pups fed when larger hunts fail. Other hunters mock him for his size and his strange croaking. He does not care. The pups do not go hungry.


[6/14/2026 3:15 PM] lizardpelt
He is deeply uncomfortable with violence. He has never been in a rend. He has never killed another wolf. He avoids border patrols and hides when Greyspire raiders come. Some call him a deerheart. He lets them.

**Backstory:**  
Croaker was born the runt of a large litter. His mother abandoned him at two moons. He survived by eating frogs and insects, hiding in hollow logs, and learning to be invisible. A Bog-Born found him, half-starved, and brought him back to the den. He has been the pack’s small-prey hunter ever since.

He has a strange gift: he can mimic the calls of frogs and birds. He uses this to lure prey close. Some wolves think it is magic. He thinks it is just practice.

**Skills:**
- **Stealth (Dexterity +4):** Can hide in a clump of reeds and not be seen from a frog-length away.
- **Hunting (Dexterity +3):** Expert at catching small, fast prey with precise paw-strikes.
- **Mimicry (Charisma +3):** Can imitate frog calls, bird chirps, and insect buzzes to lure prey.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Tiny (Strength -4):** Cannot fight. One good bite from a rival would kill him.
- **Easily Startled (Wisdom -3):** Panics at loud noises — a cracking stick, a raised voice, a monster on the thunderpath.
- **Obsessive (Wisdom -2):** Will spend all day trying to catch a single frog. Loses track of time and pack.

**RP Sample:**  
Croaker crouched on a mossy log, his speckled coat blending with the lichen. Below him, in the black water, a bullfrog called *jug-o-rum*. He called back. Same rhythm. Same pitch.

The bullfrog surfaced, confused. Croaker’s paw shot out. The frog was gone, swallowed in one gulp.

He croaked softly to himself, a satisfied, throaty sound, and scanned the water for another. A water-rat swam by. He ignored it. Today was frog day. He was very particular about his hunts.

A young wolf, a pup named Mudpup, crept up behind him. “Croaker? The alpha wants you at the border.”

Croaker jumped, nearly fell off the log, and spun around. “Don’t — don’t do that. I was *hunting*.”

Mudpup blinked. “You were eating frogs.”

“That’s hunting.” Croaker shook his head and hopped off the log. “Fine. Lead the way. But if I miss a frog because of you, you’re bringing me one from the fresh-kill cache.”

**Other:** Open to plots where his mimicry is mistaken for magic, or where he is forced into a fight he cannot win.
