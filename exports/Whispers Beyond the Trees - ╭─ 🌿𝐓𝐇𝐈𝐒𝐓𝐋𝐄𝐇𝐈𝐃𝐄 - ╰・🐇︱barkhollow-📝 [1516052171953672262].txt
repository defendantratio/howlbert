**Appearance:**

Barkhollow is a stout, round wolf with a coat of rough brown and gray — like tree bark. His fur is thick and knotted, often covered in moss and leaf litter. Her eyes are a warm, dark amber, and he has a deep, hollow-chested appearance (a birth defect that makes her lungs work harder). His paws are wide and calloused, good for digging. She smells of damp earth and rotting wood.



**Personality:**

*Slow, methodical, kind, forgetful, deeply wise.*

Barkhollow moves at his own pace. She does not hurry, does not panic, does not raise her voice. Other wolves find his slowness frustrating — but he never misses a root or a mushroom. She has never returned from a foraging trip empty-pawed.

He is forgetful, often losing track of time or leaving her herb pouch behind. But she remembers *places* — the exact location of every berry bush, every nut tree, every patch of feverfew. His memory of the land is flawless.

**Backstory:**

Barkhollow was born with a hollow chest — her lungs did not form properly. His mother expected her to die. He did not. She learned to move slowly, to conserve breath, to listen more than speak.

When he was two, she discovered a hidden grove of wintergreen berries that saved the pack from starvation during a harsh Leaf-bare. Finn’pelt gave him a name: Barkhollow, for his rough coat and her empty chest. He wears it with pride.

**RP Sample:**

Barkhollow knelt beside a patch of feverfew, her wide paws gently parting the leaves. He did not pick the plant yet. First, she bowed his head.

“Thank you, Maw, for this gift. I will take only what I need.”

Then she plucked three leaves and placed them in his pouch. Behind her, a young wolf tapped his paw impatiently.

“Barkhollow, the alpha is waiting.”

He stood slowly. “The alpha can wait. The feverfew cannot.”

She shuffled toward the den, taking his time, leaving the young wolf to sigh and follow.
