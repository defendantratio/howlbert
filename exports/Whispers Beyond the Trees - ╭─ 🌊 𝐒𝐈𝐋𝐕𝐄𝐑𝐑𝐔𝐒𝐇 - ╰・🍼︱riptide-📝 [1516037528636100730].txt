# NPC

**Name:** Riptide
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Caretaker 
**Age:** 5 years  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Riptide is a small, roundish wolf with a coat of soft gray and cream — like river stones. His fur is short and sleek, and he is always damp from wading in the shallows to fetch floating pups. His eyes are a warm, hazel brown, and he has a small white patch on his chest. His ears are slightly too large for his head, giving him a perpetually worried look. He moves with a quick, bobbing gait, never still.

**Personality:**  
*Anxious, nurturing, easily flustered, deeply loyal, secretly brave.*  

Riptide is not a natural den-keeper. He is anxious, easily overwhelmed, and cries when pups get hurt. But he is also *good* at it — he notices when a pup is sick before anyone else, he stays up all night with the colicky ones, and he has a gift for finding lost pups in the tall reeds.

He is fiercely loyal to Silverrush. He was born a Driftwood — an outsider — and the pack took him in. He will spend his life repaying that debt.

**Backstory:**  
Riptide was born to a rogue mother who died when he was a pup. He was found alone on the riverbank, half-drowned, by a Silverrush patrol. Saltmuzzle allowed him to stay. He spent his first year carrying stones, proving himself.

When the previous caretaker retired, Riptide volunteered. No one else wanted the job. He has been doing it for two years, and he still has no idea what he is doing.

**RP Sample:**  
Riptide splashed through the shallows, his heart pounding. Driftpup had wandered off again — the little scamp was always disappearing. He followed the tiny pawprints to a thick clump of reeds.

“Driftpup! Where are you?”

A small gray face poked out. “Here.”

Riptide exhaled. “Don’t do that. You scared me.”

Driftpup tilted his head. “Why?”

“Because — never mind. Just come back to the den.”

He nudged the pup back toward shore, already planning to move the den farther from the water.
