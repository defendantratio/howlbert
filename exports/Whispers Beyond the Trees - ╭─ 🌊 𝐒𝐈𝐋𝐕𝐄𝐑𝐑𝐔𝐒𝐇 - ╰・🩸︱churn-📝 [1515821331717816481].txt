# NPC

**Name:** Churn  
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Hunter  
**Age:** 5 years  
**Sexuality:** Heterosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Churn is a thick-necked, barrel-chested wolf with a coat of dark gray and white, like a storm cloud over the river. His fur is short and water-repellent, and he is always damp from swimming. His eyes are a cold, pale gray, and he has a deep, jagged scar across his shoulder (from a beaver trap). His tail is thick and muscular, used as a rudder in fast current. He moves with a rolling, powerful gait, never hurried.

**Personality:**  

Churn is the strongest swimmer in Silverrush, though he never brags. He hunts the deep channels, the parts of the river where the current could drown a wolf, and brings back the largest fish. Other hunters respect him. They do not like him. He is too quiet, too intense, too focused.

He is fiercely competitive with Aromis, the pack’s other hunter. He does not hate Aromis; he simply cannot stand being second-best. He trains in secret, pushing himself harder, hoping to catch more fish, run faster, hunt longer.

**Backstory:**  
Churn was born in Silverrush to a hunter mother and a guard father. His mother drowned when he was a pup: not in the river, but in a flash flood that swept her off a sandbar. Churn watched it happen. He has been afraid of deep water ever since.

He hides it by being the best. He swims where others dare not, not because he is brave, but because he refuses to let the river win. He hunts in memory of his mother, hoping that if he catches enough fish, the Maw will let her rest.

**Skills:**
- **Swimming (Dexterity +4):** Powerful, fast, can fight the strongest currents.
- **Hunting (Strength +3):** Specializes in large fish: salmon, pike, sturgeon.
- **Endurance (Constitution +3):** Can hold his breath for a surprising length of time.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Afraid of Deep Water (Wisdom -3):** He hides it well, but if he ever panics, he could drown.
- **Quiet (Charisma -3):** Other wolves forget he exists. He has no allies.
- **Competitive (Wisdom -2):** Might take unnecessary risks to beat Aromis.

**Maw-Belief:** Doubter — he cannot believe in a Maw that took his mother. He hunts for her, not for a god.

**RP Sample:**  
Churn stood at the river’s edge, his pale gray eyes fixed on the dark water. The current was strong today, faster than usual, but he did not hesitate. He stepped into the water and let it take him.

He swam deep, kicking with his powerful hind legs, using his tail as a rudder. Below him, a shadow moved. A sturgeon. Three deer-lengths long. He dove.

The fish sensed him and turned. Churn grabbed its tail, held on, let it drag him through the water. He did not let go. He never let go.

When the fish tired, he bit through its spine and paddled to the bank, dragging the carcass behind him. He laid it on the sand and sat, breathing hard.

Aromis appeared on the bank. “Good catch.”

Churn did not answer. He did not need praise. He needed the river to know he was not afraid.

**Other:** Open to plots where his fear is exposed, or where he and Aromis become rivals or allies.
