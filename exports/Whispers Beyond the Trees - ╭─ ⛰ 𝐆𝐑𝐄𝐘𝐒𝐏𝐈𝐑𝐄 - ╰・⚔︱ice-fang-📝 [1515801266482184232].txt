**Name:** Icefang  
**Pack:** Greyspire  
**Rank:** Beta (Stoneguard Captain)  
**Age:** 6 years  
**Sexuality:** Aromantic asexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Icefang is lean and wiry, built for speed and precision rather than brute strength. Her fur is a stark white-gray, almost luminous in moonlight, with darker gray points on her ears and tail. Her most striking feature is her *teeth*: all of them filed to sharp points, a ritual she underwent when she joined the Stoneguard. The filing has left her gums permanently raw, and she often has blood on her lips. Her eyes are a flat, pale blue that shows no emotion. She has no scars: not because she has never been wounded, but because she *surgically removes* any scar tissue to keep her pelt smooth and her movements unhindered.

**Personality:**  

Icefang does not speak unless necessary. When she does, it is in short, clipped sentences. She communicates more through posture and gesture than words. She is the perfect Stoneguard: she obeys without question, kills without hesitation, and feels nothing that would compromise her duty. Young wolves find her terrifying. Older wolves respect her. Grim trusts her, as much as he trusts anyone.

Beneath the ice, there is a strange *tenderness* for the mountains themselves. She grooms the stone, clearing lichen from sacred ledges. She murmurs to the peaks before a hunt. Her loyalty is not to Grim, but to Greyspire itself. If Grim ever betrayed the pack, she would kill him without blinking.

**RP Sample:**  
The ridge was slick with frost, but Icefang did not slow. She flowed over the ice like water, her filed teeth bared against the wind. Below her, a Greyspire patrol had cornered a Thistlehide scout, a young wolf, barely more than a pup, with terror in his eyes.

Icefang dropped from the ridge without a sound. She landed behind the Thistlehide wolf, close enough to feel his body heat.

“Move,” she said. Not to the scout. To her own patrol. They stepped back.

The scout turned. His eyes went wide at her teeth, her blood-flecked lips, her flat blue stare.

“Your alpha sent you,” Icefang said. It was not a question.

The scout nodded, trembling.

“Tell him this.” Icefang leaned in. Her breath was cold. “The mountain does not forgive. The mountain does not forget. But it is *patient*. He has until the moon is whole again to answer Grim's summons. After that…” She touched the scout’s throat with one claw, gentle as a lover. “After that, we rend with teeth.”

She turned and walked away. The scout collapsed.

**Other:** Open to plots involving her loyalty being tested, or a wolf trying to reach the person beneath the ice.
