**Appearance:**  
Skye is a striking black-and-white wolf with the heavy build of a dire wolf — broad shoulders, thick neck, and a barrel chest. Her coat is patchy black over a white undercoat, giving her a piebald appearance. Her most famous feature is her mismatched eyes: one deep green, one pale blue. Blue ribbons are woven into her fur at her shoulders, flanks, and tail — not for decoration, but to hide old scars from Twoleg traps and hunter's knives. Her ears are tattered, and her muzzle is scarred from a fire she survived as a pup. She moves with a predator’s grace, but there is a weariness in her bones.

**Personality:**  
Skye is a wolf of deep loyalties and deeper wounds. She loves late nights — the quiet, the stars, the feeling of being the only wolf awake in the world. She is fiercely protective of pups, the weak, and anyone she considers *hers*. She has a dark sense of humor and a habit of laughing at inappropriate moments — a nervous tic from years of trauma.

She is restless, unable to stay still for long. She paces borders, climbs ridges, swims rivers. She is searching for something — a place to belong, a mate, a reason to stop running. She has not found it yet.


[6/14/2026 5:34 PM] lizardpelt
**Backstory:**  
Skye was born an orphan shortly after her parents died in a wildfire — a fire caused by Twolegs clearing land. She was raised alone in the woods, stealing food from a nearby Twoleg cabin. The hunters who lived there caught her in traps, beat her, left scars she still hides with ribbons. She escaped and wandered for years, joining packs and being cast out, never fitting.

She found a true mate — or so she thought. They had pups together. Then he left, taking the pups, and the pack threw her out. She has been alone since, until Finn’pelt of Thistlehide offered her a place. She guards the borders now, watching for Greyspire raiders and Twoleg monsters. She wears her ribbons like armor.

**Skills:**
- **Fighting (Strength +4):** Brutal, experienced, fights dirty.
- **Tracking (Intelligence +3):** Can follow a scent through fire-scarred land.
- **Intimidation (Charisma +3):** Her mismatched eyes and scars make her terrifying.

**RP Sample:**  
Skye sat on a mossy rock at the Thistlehide border, her mismatched eyes scanning the treeline. The blue ribbons in her fur fluttered in the evening breeze. She was alone — she was always alone — and she liked it that way.

A twig snapped. She was on her paws instantly, teeth bared.

A young scout, a nervous male named Mossheart, emerged from the ferns. “Skye. Finn’pelt wants you at the den.”

“Tell him I’ll come when I’m done watching.” She did not sit back down. She never sat when she could stand.

Mossheart hesitated. “He said it was urgent.”

Skye turned her mismatched eyes on him. He flinched. Good.

“Fine.” She leaped off the rock and padded past him, her ribbons trailing like battle flags. “But if I miss a Greyspire raider because of this, I’m eating your share of fresh-kill.”

**Other:** Open to plots involving her lost pups, a new mate, or her scars being revealed.
