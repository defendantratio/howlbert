# NPC

**Name:** Mosspup  
**Pack:** Mistmoor  
**Rank:** Pup  
**Age:** 6 moons  
**Sexuality:** Too young  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Female (she/her)

**Appearance:**  
Mosspup is a small, lean pup with a coat of pale tan and soft green — like dead cattails and fresh algae. Her fur is short and water-repellent, and she is always damp. Her eyes are a warm, honey-brown, and she has a small, dark spot on her nose that looks like a leaf. Her ears are large and expressive, and she has a habit of flattening them when she is thinking. Her tail is thin and whip-like.

**Personality:**  
*Curious, talkative, empathetic, easily frightened, desperately eager to help.*  

Mosspup asks questions. Constantly. *Why is the water warm? Where does the mist come from? What does the Maw taste like?* Her mother is exhausted. Other pups avoid her (she talks too much). She does not care. She wants to *understand*.

She is deeply empathetic, often crying when another pup falls or when a hunter comes back with an empty mouth. She wants to be a healer when she grows up — like Mirewort, but *happier*. She collects interesting leaves and mushrooms, pressing them between stones to dry.

**Backstory:**  
Mosspup was born in Mistmoor to a Bog-Born mother and a scout father. Both are still alive, though her father is often away on patrol. She is the eldest of three surviving pups; her younger siblings are healthy and loud.

She nearly died of rot-lung when she was three moons old. Mirewort saved her with a poultice of marsh-mallow root and feverfew. She has followed him around ever since, asking questions, watching his paws, trying to learn. He pretends to be annoyed. She knows he is not.

**Skills:**
- **Herblore (Intelligence +2):** Surprisingly good at identifying plants for her age.
- **Medicine (Wisdom +2):** Can mimic basic treatments she has watched.

**RP Sample:**  
Mosspup crouched beside Mirewort, watching his paws as he ground a bundle of dried leaves into powder. She did not speak. She had learned that he worked better in silence. But her tail twitched with the effort of holding back questions.

“You want to ask something,” he said without looking up.

“Is it true that the Maw talks to you in dreams?”

His paws paused. Then he continued grinding. “Sometimes.”

“What does it say?”

He did not answer for a long moment. When he did, his voice was quiet. “It says I am not alone.”

Mosspup did not understand. But she nodded and filed the answer away. She would figure it out when she was older.

**Other:**  
- **Family:** Mother — Bog-Born named Sedge; Father — Scout named Whisper.  
- **Open plots:** Mirewort’s apprentice in training; possible illness that tests her skills; her curiosity could lead her into danger.
