# NPC

**Name:** Pebble  
**Pack:** Silverrush  
**Rank:** Diplomat  
**Age:** 3 years  
**Sexuality:** Homosexual  
**Gender/Pronouns:** Male (he/him)

**Appearance:**  
Pebble is a small, roundish wolf with a coat of soft gray and cream, like river stones smoothed by centuries of current. His fur is short and sleek, and he is always damp; not from swimming, but from nervous sweating. His eyes are a bright, nervous hazel, darting constantly. He has a small, crooked scar on his left hind leg (a birth defect corrected by surgery as a pup), and his ears are slightly too large for his head, giving him a perpetual look of surprise.

**Personality:**  
*Anxious, meticulous, earnest, surprisingly brave, deeply lonely.*  

Pebble is not a natural diplomat. He stutters when he is nervous (which is always), forgets his talking points, and apologizes too much. But he is *good* at the job — not because he is smooth, but because he is honest. Wolves trust him because he is too anxious to lie convincingly.

He was chosen by Saltmuzzle because he is the only wolf in Silverrush who volunteers for the role. No one else wants it. So Pebble stumbles through negotiations, making mistakes, learning from them, and somehow,  against all odds,  getting results.

**Backstory:**  
Pebble was born with a twisted leg. The pack's healer said he would never walk properly. He proved them wrong, after three surgeries (bite-and-hold corrections, painful and slow), he could run. He was never fast, but he was *steady*.

He became a diplomat because he was not strong enough to hunt, not fast enough to scout, not brave enough to guard. He thought he would fail. Saltmuzzle gave him a chance. He has been failing upward ever since.

**Weaknesses:**
- **Anxious (Charisma -3):** Stutters, sweats, forgets. Undermines his own authority.
- **Lonely (Wisdom -3):** Desperately wants friends. Could be manipulated with kindness.
- **Crooked Leg (Dexterity -3):** Cannot run. If a negotiation turns violent, he is dead.

**RP Sample:**  
Pebble stood at the border, his too-large ears swiveling. The Greyspire envoy, a massive female named Sleet, stared down at him. He tried not to tremble.

"You wanted to discuss the fishing rights," he said. His voice cracked. He cleared his throat. "The — the stretch of river near the mill ruins. It used to be neutral. But the fish are back now, and both our packs want access."

Sleet said nothing. Pebble kept going.

"I propose a — a rotation. One week Greyspire, one week Silverrush. Alternating. Starting with you. To show good faith."

Silence. Then Sleet inclined her head. "Acceptable."

Pebble blinked. "Really?"

"Do you want me to change my mind?"

"No! No. Thank you." He backed away, nearly tripping over a root. "I will — I will bring the agreement to my alpha. Thank you."

He fled. Behind him, Sleet almost smiled.

**Other:** Open to plots where his anxiety is exploited, or where he must negotiate under threat of violence.
